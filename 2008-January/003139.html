Assuming OpenIMS follows the 3GPP specification here, then you'll find info
on this in Annex F of 3GPP TS 29.228
(http://www.3gpp.org/ftp/Specs/html-info/29228.htm).
 
According to V7.6.0 the regular expression format is specified in "Extended
REgular Expressions" as defined in chapter 9 in IEEE 1003.1-2004 Part 1.
