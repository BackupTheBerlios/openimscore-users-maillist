Hello Sabyasachi,
 
> 1. Sometimes received 0A0D from OpenIMS.

This are the NAT-ping messages. If you do not need to keep NAT-pinholes open
than you can disable this messages by seeting the "ping flag" to zero
	modparam("pcscf","ping", 0)
in your pcscf.cfg


> 2. Even if orig UE received the response (183) against the INVITE request,
> Open IMS retransmitting the INVITE request to term UE.

I do not see INVITEs retransmitted but a lot of PRACK requests which are not
answered by your client. Look at the szenario trace which I have attached.


In case there are further issues it would be helpful to have a full trace
including the internal messages, not only the user agent side.
