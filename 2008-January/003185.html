Andreas Häber wrote:
> Hi,
>  
> Different services are specified & standardized by different fora/bodies.
>  
> For Presence you may refer to 3GPP TS 22.141 (requirements), TS 23.141
> (architecture & functional description) and TS 24.141 (details). See
> http://www.3gpp.org/ftp/Specs/html-info/23-series.htm for a list of
> 3GPP specs. Read through the list of titles and you should find lot's
> of different services. Also see the Open IPTV Forum
> (http://www.openiptvforum.org/ (IPTV)) and the Open Mobile Alliance
> (OMA) (http://www.openmobilealliance.org/ (mobile)) as well for more
> services.
>  
> Implementation of services depends on the service itself, but usually
> just normal software engineering. You should be able to find some open
> source implementations around through Google, especially for Presence
> (like http://www.openser.org/dokuwiki/doku.php/presence:presence-module).
>  
> Hope this helps!
