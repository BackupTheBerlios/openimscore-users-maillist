Hi Tomasz,


> Unfortunately, the Request-URI can not be compared with a regular
> expression. This is what the 3GPP standards indicate, although I would
> agree that an optional regular expression checking would be very beneficial.
> 

UCT IMS IPTV, in its How-To Guide says that you should trigger with

- Sip Header = To
- Sip Header Content = *the expression you want*

because you can't use Request-URI.

I hope it helps
