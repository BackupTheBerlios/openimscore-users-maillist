Hello Dragos,

Thanx for the clarification. I have a doubt regarding the current
implementation. As you said Pings are sent by PCSCF to UE at regular
interval. But as per "draft-ietf-sip-outbound-14" draft, Ping (CR LF CR
LF) should be sent by UE and after receiving the Ping, server will respond
through Pong (CR LF).

So in current implementation if user has ping=1 in the pcscf.cfg then PCSCF
will wait for ping if received then send pong else do nothing.

Please correct me if i am wrong.

Sabyasachi
