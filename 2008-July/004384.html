Hi Fernando,
 
> Not know if it is relevant however ims-communicator is asking twice for
> password when trying to register.

The problem is well known (qop-issue) and thanks Dragos we have as a
workaround coming with r575 of OpenIMSCore the possibility to deactivate qop
mechanism at registration. This is necessary as long as the IMS communicator
has this issue (which will probably be solved soon).  

Meanwhile you should yous fresh r575 and then set the new parameter in
scscf.cfg: modparam("scscf","registration_qop",""). This should not fill any
qop in the challenge.
