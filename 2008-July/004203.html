Hi Markus,

Just look in the icscf.cfg and change the checks there to include your 
new initial request. This behavior is completely user-configurable from 
that script. Subsequent requests should not come at the I-CSCF, as it 
never Record-routes on the initial request of a dialog. So you should 
not send for example NOTIFY or BYE to the I-CSCF.
