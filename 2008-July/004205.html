you don't necessarily need to wait, but if you do, it might help speed 
things up. The reason is that any Diameter peers would try on connection 
to connect out. It's a chicken and the egg problem for who started first 
and at least one side would fail to connect as the other one is not 
started yet, but the other one would make it. Now the thing is that if 
it fails, the next attempt would be in 30 seconds... which might seem a 
looong time when you are in a hurry to try something...
