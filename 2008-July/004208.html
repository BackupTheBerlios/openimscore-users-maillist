> The trace is not fully clear for m. There are a few packets missing due to
> IP packet fragmentation. You can avoid this by using tcpdump with a negative
> filter, e.g. tcpdump -i any -s 3000 -w /var/tmp/trace.pcap not port 22 and
> not port 3306.
>
> I guess your problem is with upper/lower case of username which is case
> sensitive in SIP. Bob has registered with username "Bob" and the session is
> setup to user "bob". These are two different users, but a transport
> association for "bob" cannot be found at P-CSCF.
>
> So I propose you should do the test again with identical usernames.
