Hi Naveen,
 
you may replace all the 127.0.0.1 to your IP address in files below:
 
icscf.cfg, icscf.xml, pcscf.cfg, pcscf.xml, scscf.cfg, scscf.xml, and DiameterPeerHSS.xml.
 
If your client on the different system want to use the FQDN of p-cscf, you also need to change DNS settings such as resolv.conf and open-ims.dnszone.
