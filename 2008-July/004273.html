Hi Mustafa,

You can use the Open IMS Core for research without issues. The Open IMS 
Core is entirely GPLv2, just like SER, and as such no usage restrictions 
can be enforced.

However, be aware of multiple patents on IMS technologies and the fact 
that the authors of the Open IMS Core can not protect you if with your 
usage you are infringing on them.

Cheers,
-Dragos
