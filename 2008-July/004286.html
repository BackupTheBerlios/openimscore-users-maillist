Hi Daniel,

> I see that the presence subscriptions and publish methods are sent to 
the
> AS which forwards them back to SCSCF back and forth and finally PCSCF
> sends them to UE.
> 
> Why is this so?

This you have to investigate with your presence server. My guess is that 
the
presence server first honours the Route header and therefore forwards the
SUBSCRIBE and PUBLISH request back to S-CSCF.
See attached scenario-trace.

> What I understand is that
> 1) Subscribe messages should be taken care by AS - if so why Request URI
> is not changed by SCSCF?

Because requests are forwarded to an AS via the Route (2 Routes are 
inserted
by S-CSCF) header without changing the request URI.

> 2) Publish messages should be sent to UE - for this OK

No. PUBLISH requests should be sent to the presence server, which should
store the state-information and send NOTIFYs if requested.
