Asterisk hosts applications like voicemail. It would be an advantage for
OpenIMSCore to re-use existing applications. Regarding routing capabilities
there is of course an overlap in functions, but from IMS point of view the
routing engine is the IMS platform. Asterisk should deliver only
applications.
