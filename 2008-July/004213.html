Hi Marco,

The Open IMS Core as of now does not process any media related data. 
Only signaling. So this is between the UE and the AS.

The only case is when the P-CSCF does that RTPProxy thing for NAT... but 
even then, it's just just a proxy of RTP packets without any intrusion.
