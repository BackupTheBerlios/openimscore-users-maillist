Hi all,

As the developer of most of the functionalities that you're listing 
first of all I would like to thank you for your good review ;), but as 
Dragos said before I would use Asterisk only as ISDN breakout gateway 
(voice or videocalls) but I would not use it as an application server 
(for voicemail, video ivr, transcoding, etc).

In fact I implemented my latest development, a MCU, as separate servers 
(Media Server + Sailfin Application) and I was thinking in porting all 
the rest of functionalities outside of Asterisk into the Media Server.

Do you think it could be interesting? Here is more information of what 
I've developed so far and could be integrated as an application

http://sip.fontventa.com/

Best regards
Sergio
