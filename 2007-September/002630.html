>
> First of all I would say that RTPProxy is enabled in P-CSCF and that
> both are connected.
>
> The problem arises during codec negotiation as you would expect. If
> the negotiation was Invite, Ack, 100, 180 , 200, there would not have

> in first 2 messages containing SDP offer and not in subsequent

>
> My question is: does the P-CSCF sends all messages with SDP offer to
> RTPProxy? In this case the bug would be in RTPProxy. Otherwise I think
> that it would be in P-CSCF.
