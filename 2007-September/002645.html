The SUBSCRIBE(expires:3600) request can be forwarded to AS through P-CSCF -> S-CSCF -> AS. But this doesn't 
happen when sending UNSUBSCRUBE request. The reason why the SUBSCRIBE(expires: 0) request is not forwarded 
to AS may be from S-CSCF. From the notification of S-CSCF, it shows an error "s_save_dialog: dialog already exists!".
It seems that this error cause the failure of UNSUBSCRIBE request forwarding. That is why UNSUBSCRIBE request 
(frame29) stops at S-CSCF instead of forwarding further.

And, the service profile, trigger points and iFC are by default. I just modify the AS Service name to my IP addr from 
web console.
