Hi Amol Pise,

if you use wireshark: right button on the entry, "decode as...", then the "transport" tab and (port should be already 3869/3870) search for "DIAMETER".

I think the reason is 3868 is the usual DIAMETER port. to avoid a conflict by having the same port they always used +1. you will also not find the usual sip-port 3060 on osims (but 4060,5060,6060)...
But I'm not realy shure about that point, maybe you find something on openimscore-hp...

br, Klemens
