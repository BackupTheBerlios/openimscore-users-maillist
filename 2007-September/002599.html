Hello Sudhakar,

No, it's not a bug. You requested "CableLabs-Digest" and that is the
identifier for the Digest authentication from CableLabs. If you want to
get a Unknown identifier, then please select HSS-Selected.

Also please be aware that the NASS-Bundled authentication is still not
in a trunk, but in a branch. Will be merged soon.

-Dragos
