I haven't used X-Lite on Linux but, on Windows, the "Awaiting for proxy
login info" message means that X-Lite doesn't have enough information to
even try contacting the proxy. I suspect you should remove the "sip:"
prefixes from the proxy and outbound proxy configurations.

This probably has nothing to do with OpenIMSCore ... just yet ;-)
