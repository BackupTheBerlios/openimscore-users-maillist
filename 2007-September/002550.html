Hi!

As fas as I know there are some IMS models (
www.cost290.org/td2006/tds/td06014.pdf) for the OPNET simulation tool , and
that is the difference to openims, while OPNET is good for simulation
purposes openims is a  implementation of the core IMS elements that enables
you to test your own network elements (user equipment, application servers,
gateways, hss) with the openims elements for compatibility or performance
checks.

It will depend on your goals whether OPNET simulations or an openims
environment is more suitable.
