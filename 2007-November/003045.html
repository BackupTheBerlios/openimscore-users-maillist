I don't know any specific SIP client that supports this, but also, I
never looked.
That is the standard way according to IMS specs, and it's called
P-CSCF discovery (3GPP TS 24.229 §9.2.1 points to RFC3361 for IPv4 and
RFC3319 for IPv6, and ETSI TISPAN incorporates this in the NACF
function of the NASS).
Then there are some clients or phones out there that support other
dynamic provisioning schemes... but I don't know the details.
