Hi,
yes attached RTP stream was  plain, but because my microphone layed on 
the table :). But also in the case, I was speaking, and the RTP was not 
plain, I was not able to hear anything. The problem disappeared after 
restarting both computers, running OpenIC. I think, there might be some 
problems with concurrent access of more applications to audio devices. 
One direction of RTP stream was caused by the incorrect firewall 
settings, while routing between two networks. Now everything works fine.

I have one more questions:
1) How can I set the preferred audio codec in OpenIC? The bandwidth of 
G.711 (approx. 83kbps over RTP) is too wide. I would like to use a codec 
with wider bandwidth. 
2) However using G.711 (Mean opinion score > 4) the the audio quality is 
poor. Does someone have the same experience?
3) Was OpenIC tested for interoperability with other IMS or SIP clients?

Thanx for comments in advance.
