> well yes, you can can resolve it, but to 10.200.200.200. Then you have
> configured your JavaDiameterPeer stack to listen on:
>
>    <Acceptor port="3868" bind="10.200.220.200" />
>
> which is not the same... 
>
> if you want to listen on all interfaces, just don't specify the bind
> parameter.
>
> also, very helpful to debug this issue (I think just this week I asked
> it for 10 times) is to start a wireshark and see where the TCP
> connection attempt is actually going, if it is going anywhere. Then
> compare that with an output from netstat -nlp .
