Hi Amol,

Be "secure your AS" I meant that you need to extend your AS to
differentiate and reject. The simple and unsafe way is to see if the
message was received over the ISC interface and as such the message have
the ISC mark. However, this is unsecure and also specific to the Open
IMS Core.

The nice way is to have a Sh implementation into your AS and also check
if the user has the proper IFC attached or not.

BTW: at one point there were some people interested in implementing the
Sh interface into our ser_ims base. That would be helpful here, although
I do not recommend to use SER as an Application Server...

-Dragos
