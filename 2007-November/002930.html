So, the current work is in the 08_cdp_sessions. Docs could be generated
by doing:

cd <ser_ims_branch>/doxygen
doxygen doxygen.config

But... I think that it is easier to take a look at
modules/cdp/session.c,h and authstatemachine.c,h . acctstatemachine has
to be written. Then there is an acc_rf module in 05_cdp_sessions that
would need to be adapted.

BTW: This week on Thursday we have a kind of developer meeting during
the 3rd FOKUS Workshop. Most of the people working on this would meet
then (Joao who did the acc_rf and acctstatemachine in the 05 branch,
Alberto who is working on the 08, me, a.s.o). If you'll be joining we'll
have a great opportunity to talk.
