Hi Mikeal, hi Fwind,

the FOKUS XDMS (there Mikael is right) is closed source and you can
license it for usage inside of testbed (meaning non-commercial)
environments - either the binaries or the source code. 

It does not come with NDAs etc. attached and for details on its
structure and implementation and for obtaining the software, please use
xdms at open-ims.org and please don't use this mailing list.

Sorry for the FOKUS marketing throw-in ;)

- Peter
