A loop. most probably you have a wrong Request-URI in the REGISTER
message. The correct one is sip:open-ims.test, or whatever your domain
is, and not sip:pcscf.open-ims.test:4060, because you are not
registering to the P-CSCF but to the domain.

Anyway, if still in trouble, please send a trace... else it's just
guessing...
