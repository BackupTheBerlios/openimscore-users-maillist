Hi,

First, if SEMS + SER provides you with what you need, then why worry?

However, IMS can provide value with QoS, addressing (multi-operator,
multi-xyz), service infrastructure, AAA, and probably lot's more. If those
are important for you then IMS can help you out... For example, if you need
a charging infrastructure for your conference and also multiple networks are
involved.

For most people, IMS is an overkill to be used. How many people run their
own GSM core network?

There are many articles written about the wonders of IMS, such as
http://www.ericsson.com/technology/tech_articles/IMS.shtml from Ericsson.
Also see http://theimslantern.blogspot.com/ which has lot's of good articles
about the IMS (start at the tail
(http://theimslantern.blogspot.com/2007/04/why-dedicating-blog-to-ims.html)
and work your way towards the head...)

Best regards,
Andreas
