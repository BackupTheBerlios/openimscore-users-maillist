Hi Kiran,


Probably not very IMS, but you can check my applications for Asterisk:
http://sip.fontventa.com

They would allow you to integrate any streaming server (for example a 
DarwingStreamingServer or even an Axis camera) and play the contents
to a softphone connected with a SIP call (DTMF interaction is also implemented
to change videos through asterisk dialplan).

Also you can find a H324M library to create a 3G<->SIP gateway with asterisk.

Best regards and hope you find it interesting
