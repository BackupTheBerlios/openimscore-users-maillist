Hi,

well we have some B2B-UAs acting as the P-CSCF (which are distributed
throughout germany). The clients register every 10 minutes or so at
P-CSCF, which will then itself register every 7 days or so at the
core. This reduces the traffic significantly.
While we are now only rebuilding the existing voice-infrastructure
with IMS, the idea behind it is definitely to add more
value-added-services to our network at a later stage.
