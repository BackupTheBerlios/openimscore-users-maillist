Dear Sir ,

That means, current IMS architecture is going to be installed in without any
architectural change? For example in spain it's still lot of issues of
ambiguity of going for its full fledged implementation. I have talked with
some people from France Telecom and they say they are also not very sure for
going for it's full fledged implementation.

Now if German Operators are going for implementation , i guess this
signalling issues are like this as it is now. Keeping this point in mind , i
would like to know as a general query is that, regarding IMS what are the
hot topics which are bothering the industry as a whole? I mean , is it the
signalling related issues concerning its complex architecture or issues
related to IMS based various kind of next generation Value Added Services?
If anything else is going high also ? I would also like to get some thoughts
in this point is that , if a new less complex IMS architecture comes up with
less signalling related load will that make any positive impact on the
industry and research communities?

Regarding OpenIMSCore , well i guess it's still not fully 3GPP compliant, i
may be wrong also.

> >

> present

> >
> > What my thought on this matter is, amongst all the various elements the
> core

> > unnecessary which gives rise to so many signalling messages , be it SIP
> or

> > much more simple core having maybe 1 or 2 proxy servers maximum along
