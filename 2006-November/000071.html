Yes, it does work wonderfully from the command line. And I would highly 
recommend using the command line as it is easier than it seems.

you need to do first a:

svn checkout 
http://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/openimscore/ser_ims/trunk <dest_dir>

then when you want to see what we changed, just do a:
svn diff <dest_dir> -r HEAD

and to update:
svn update <dest_dir>
