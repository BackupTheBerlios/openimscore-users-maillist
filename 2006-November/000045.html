Hi Justino,

The REGISTER message is entering in a loop because its RequestUri is "
sip:open-ims.test:3060" instead of "sip:open-ims.test".

I don't know linphone user interface, but you should configure your
server/registrar as open-ims.test and your outbound proxy as
open-ims.test:3060.
