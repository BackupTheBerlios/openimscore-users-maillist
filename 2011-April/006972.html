Hi Rosa,

just enter the database:
- mysql -uroot -p<Enter>
- In the mySQL-Shell: "drop database hss_db;", afterwards "quit"
- then "mysql -uroot -p < /opt/OpenIMSCore/FHoSS/scripts/hss_db.sql"
- then "mysql -uroot -p < /opt/OpenIMSCore/FHoSS/scripts/userdata.sql"
That should work...
