Hi Faisal,

> Is there any SIP trunk on IMS and OpenIMS? Where I
> must configure the Open IMS to make the SIP Trunking?

No. There is no specific feature implemented in OpenIMS for SIP Trunking
also called Business Trunking in ETSI TISPAN standards.
Peering based business trunking according to ETSI TS 182 025 §6.2 should
always be possible in OpenIMSCore.
Subscription based business trunking ETSI TS 182 025 §6.1 is not available
in OpenIMSCore. This requires e.g. support of wildcarded PUIDs but it would
be an interesting enhancement (http://www.openimscore.org/node/132 ).
