Hi Amit,

the ser_ims usually relays messages with just a few milliseconds of
delay. So I would just use the system's time for timestamping the ACR.
Also I would recommend to enable a ntp-client on your IMS systems, just
to keep your time in sync with other nodes. If you really want, you
could add a Date header, but I would rather not.

In case you would read the timestamps from the messages, I wouldn't
trust the clients or other nodes to produce that information...

Cheers,
-Dragos
