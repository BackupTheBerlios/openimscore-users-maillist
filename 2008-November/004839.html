Hi Björn,

> Registration with Digest AKA works, and initially the P-CSCF receives ESP
> encapsulated messages correctly. Then it suddenly stops accepting the ESP
> packets, and "setkey -D" shows that the security associations have been
> removed. This usually happens within a few minutes after registration and
> makes my testing rather difficult.
> 
> Does anyone know what could be causing this? 

I do not have too much experience with IPsec but the following hint might be
useful:

The security associations are setup with the help of setkey commands. You
can find the scripts used for this in
/opt/OpenIMSCore/ser_ims/modules/pcscf.

There is the possibility to specify the hard/soft life time duration of the
security associations with the -lh and -ls extension of the setkey command.

Maybe that helps.
