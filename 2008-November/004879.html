Hello Noriko,
 
> We added two AS to the same domain in openimscore and attached two
> iFCs to the service profile. so each iFC get different priority.
> 
> I feel little bit strange about it, because AS have no priority in their
> function.
> But I don't know other way to do this. I missed something ?

I do not see clear what you need.
You can only add a priority on iFC level to control the sequence of iFCs
within a service profile. Each iFC has an AS assigned.

If you want some kind of loads-haring on AS level for ASes caring for the
same service then you can do that via DNS SRV records.
