> I am new to IMS and learning the details. I have setup OpenIMS in my lab
> and have got handheld devices registered and talking to each other through
> WLAN. In what way can I setup session mobility. Please provide pointers.

Mobility in mobile networks is handled in IP-Layer (mobile IP). In that case
the client always uses the same IP address irrespective of his location.
Therefore we have no impact of mobility in IMS signalling.
