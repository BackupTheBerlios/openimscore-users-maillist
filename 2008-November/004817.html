Hi Veena,

v1 is specified in RFC3310
v2 in RFC4169, which is a short delta specifications to the 3310.

in a nutshell, v1 says that the password for MD5 is the AKA RES, while
v2 says that it is PRF(RES||IK||CK, "http-digest-akav2-password").

Just this morning somebody pointed to me that our v2 implementation is
wrong, as we implemented it based on an old draft, which indicated the
password as just RES||IK||CK....
