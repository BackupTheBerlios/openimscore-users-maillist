Hi Pedro,

> Is this behaviour normal?

Yes. The terminating user may have subscribed to services for unregisterd
users (e.g. forwarding to voicemail) it needs an S-CSCF to handle it.
Therefore an S-CSCF has to be assigned. For further details see 3GPP TS
23.228 § 5.12.1.

> How can we avoid this behaviour?

Not at all.
