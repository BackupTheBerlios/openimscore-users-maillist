> > 3) I wanted to know more about their functions (what kind of

> IETF, see RFC4740.

I assume that with "the yellow IMS book" Dragos refer to the following one:
G. Camarillo and M. A. García-Martín, The 3G IP multimedia subsystem (IMS) :
merging the Internet and the cellular worlds, 2nd ed. Chichester: Wiley,
2006.

See http://eu.wiley.com/WileyCDA/WileyTitle/productCd-0470018186.html for
TOC and further information.

It is a very, very helpful book to understand the basics of IMS, and
therefore highly recommended reading. After reading it you can look at the
various specifications and standards for more up-to-date info and the exact
details.
