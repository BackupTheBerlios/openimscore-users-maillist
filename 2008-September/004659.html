Hi Rishikesh,

> Is this problem in ims-communicator or in ims core?

Maybe it is the problem which Miguel points to (you unfortunately did not
add a tracefile to securely confirm this issue). 
The problem Miguel mentioned is on the IMS communicator side and it has its
root in the nasty "qop" problem.

But there is an easy workaround to be configured in scscf.cfg.

Just use:
   modparam("scscf","registration_qop","")
instead of 
   modparam("scscf","registration_qop","auth,auth-int")
