1) DNS... general specifications are in 3GPP TS24.229

2) look in RFC3608. The S-CSCF and potentially other addresses are
communicated to the P-CSCF and client in the 200 OK for the REGISTER, in
the Service-Route headers.

3) it's quite a lot :) of information that you need. I can only
recommend now the yellow IMS book, TS 29.228 and 29.229. If you favor
IETF, see RFC4740.

Cheers,
