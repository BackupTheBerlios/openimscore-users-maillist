Hello alvan,

It's not a bug. If you de-register nicely the subscription would be kept
something like 30 seconds more so that you will still receive the last
de-registration notifications. If you do not de-register nicely, then
your old registration is still valid and you will be notified until it
expires or is deleted. Of course each NOTIFY caries the updated dialog
period. If you just restart the client, the 481 is normal as your client
does not keep track anymore of that old subscription dialog. To prevent
this, your client should always de-register nicely.

-Dragos
