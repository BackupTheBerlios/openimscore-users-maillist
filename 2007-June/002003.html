Hi Rajeswari,

The private identity must be in a NAI format. While now it might work
with just numbers, the specs demand this and in future there might be
something that will break this. Then the public identity has to be a SIP
or TEL uri. The core uses the information in the domain part of URIs to
route messages, so you can't get around not using it. If you still think
that it should work without, please send us a trace and we will try and
explain what goes wrong and where.

-Dragos
