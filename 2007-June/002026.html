Hi Kevin,

The idea was that any initial request would be sent through the
Service-Route. As such the PUBLISH will go from UE to P-CSCF and then to
the S-CSCF for the originating user. That S-CSCF usually has an Initial
Filter Criteria that fwd the message to the presence AS. As such, the
PUBLISH won't ever get to the I-CSCF. The only case when it could, is
the inter-domain case... I can only see the case of peer-to-peer
presence, without an AS when this would be needed... well, I added also
in svn the PUBLISH method as acceptable for this case, but if you say
that you use a presence app server, then you clearly have a routing issue.

-Dragos
