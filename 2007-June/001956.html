Hello Sergey,

For let's say 1 million users and about 1-2Kbytes of info stored per
user you need about 1-2 GBytes of RAM. That's something that you have
now on normal workstation, so I wouldn't worry about this for now. Then
if you think practical in real situations for reliability/availability
reasons you would probably split and distributed the million users
between different S-CSCF anyway.

-Dragos
