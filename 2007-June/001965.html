Yes, it is possible. What you want is to have multiple IFCs in the same
service profile. Each IFC has exactly one AS associated, a trigger point
and a priority. The forwarding is sequential until one AS responds with
a final response or one fails to answer in any way and the default
behavior is to terminate the request.

-Dragos
