Hello Li,

Well, the idea behind SIP was to have an extensible protocol. So if we
demand from the APIs that for every method out-there there must be a
doNewmethod(), then it would a pain to extend the protocol and implement
new services without changing the APIs.

And about standardizing services, well, they are sort of. Look for IETF
RFCs and OMA specifications. What happens in the APIs is probably just a
matter of leaving the doors open for future extensions.

-Dragos
