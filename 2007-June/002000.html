Hi Stefan,

I think that the problem in packet 29 is that the Route header is
missing. The Route should be the reversed Record-Route from packet 22
(simpleFindMe, mt at scscf, mt at pcscf) and the Request-URI should be contact
in the same packet 22, so that mt at pcscf would know where to fwd the ACK
next.
