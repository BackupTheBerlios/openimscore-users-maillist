guess you have a problem with access permissions in your mysql database.

Look at the user privileges on hss_db database and if they are granted
against the right hostname. 
A further hint: In hss_db.sql you can find as the last statement:
grant delete,insert,select,update on hss_db.* to hss at localhost identified by
'hss';
