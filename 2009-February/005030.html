Hi Devdutt,

> I am implementing a scenario where I forward all packets coming to the IMS
> core to the sailfin AS. The sailfin AS then sends out an INVITE to another
> UE via the IMScore.
> I need that all INVITE and BYE messages get forwarded to the AS as I need
> to keep track of sessions that the AS is responsible for.
> 
> Right now on the HSS console on the iFC/Trigger page (in the Method
> option) I can only see INVITE, REGISTER, CANCEL etc. I dont see a BYE.
> However I also need to see BYE messages and forward them to the AS so that
> the AS can terminate the sessions that were started on behalf of the
> earlier INVITEs.
> 
> Is there a way this can be done. I am using an older version on the IMS
> core. Is this possible with a newer version ?

You are faced with protocol rules of SIP and the IMS architecture. It is not
allowed to change the route within a dialog. Therefore if you want to see a
BYE also at the AS you have to set Record-Route header field at the AS.

Franz
