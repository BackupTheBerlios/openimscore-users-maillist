Service Route problem, as mentioned in the 400 *Bad Request *status-line in
your trace.
Ensure your client sends a service route or try to disable the check for the
same in pcscf.cfg (enable variant2)

http://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/openimscore-users/2008-December/006367.html

        if (!P_follows_service_routes()){
                #Variant 1 - deny access to the network
                sl_send_reply("400","Bad Request - Not following indicated
service routes");
                break;
                #Variant 2 - enforce routes and let the dialog continue
                #P_enforce_service_routes();
        }
