Hi,

 

I think the problem may be caused by not successfully de-registering  Alice
before your test.

When you look at packet 74 (REGISTER) and 79 (200 OK on REGISTER) you will
see that S-CSCF includes 2 contact addresses where "192.168.1.121:51125"
stems from a registration not valid anymore.

This leads to forking where one address is unresponsive.

 

I suggest you repeat the test with a clean registration state.
