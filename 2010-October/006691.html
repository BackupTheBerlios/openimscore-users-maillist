Let me guess what your problem is:
- You are using a WLANrouter
- You try to register on your OpenIMSCore platform
- You have not told your mobile to use your specific DNS server, therefor it
cannot find the address of the of your x-CSCF.

You have to change the DHCP parameters of your WLAN router to use your
specific DNS server-. Otherwise the public DNS server is used by the mobile.
