Hello Franz,

The media description are well. Unforntunately during our development on the Rx interface the compatibility between the OpenIMSCore and PCRF from the University of Capetown was lost. We have encouraged the users to ask kindly the team from the Capetown Uni to update their PCRF as we added the port numbers in the flows.

If anybody wants to fix the compatibility between the two projects by modifying the PCRF or the P-CSCF (e.g. adding some configure params to preserve the compatibility) is free to do so.

Best regards,
Ancuta
