Hi Arnold,

I don't think that the specs allow for a regular expression in the
request uri. They do though for header name, header value and sdp line
value.

However, this what you want to do looks like wildcarded PSIs. We are in
a big process of upgrading the HSS structures and also the CSCFs to
3GPP's Rel.7 and although normal PSIs are there now, wild-carded ones
would be added in this process.

-Dragos
