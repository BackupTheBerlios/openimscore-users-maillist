Hi Asma,

ping pcscf.192.168.100.102?? As I see you configure pcscf.open-ims.test
and not pcscf.192.168.100.102...

The errors regard reverse-DNS. This is no provided as it is mainly
dependent on the IP network of the user. In your case, you will need to
add a new zone file, for 100.168.192.in-addr.arpa and there add 102 as
pointing to pcscf.open-ims.test. Anyway, this error should not cause you
any big trouble.

-Dragos
