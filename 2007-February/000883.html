Summary: P-CSCF corrupts auth header if nonce contains ck= or ik=

Details: In registration.c, function P_remove_ck_ik() corrupts the auth header in case the included nonce has a "ck=" or "ik=".

This happens because the function, while trying to remove the actual CK and IK parameters, completely ignores other parameters and their quoted values, and just searches for a "ck=" or "ik=" substring in the complete header, even within other parameter values.
