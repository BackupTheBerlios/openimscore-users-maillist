Did you also configured the peers to connect to each-other? At least one
of them has to have the other in the peer list and the other to accept
also unknown peers (if you don't want to configure it reciprocal).
"cartman" is the name of my laptop (because it's fat and likes to
act-up). You can replace that with anything that you like and would also
be DNS resolvable. So use real Fully Qualified Domain Names for those
entries.

-Dragos
