X-Lite is the SIP (not IMS!) client which gives us the most headaches...
As it is closed source, we can't do anything about it being broken in
many ways. Here we have almost banned it ;-).

the registration expiration should be client configurable. The S-CSCF
enforces some sane limits - if too big it will limit it or too small it
will refuse the registration.
