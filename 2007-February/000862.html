This HSS does send MAA as per 3GPP TS 29.229. Hence
SIP-Auth-Data-Item is filled in appropriately. OSIMS
seems to be ok interpreting the AVPs in the MAA, at
least I have not seen any errors. So looks like and as
you pointed out, the issue is on how S-CSCF and HSS
share the responsibilities of Digest authentication. 

So does FHoSS send the "password" in the MAA response?
