Summary: P-CSCF sometimes corrupts auth header while removing CK/IK

Details: In registration.c, function P_remove_ck_ik() corrupts the auth
header in case the included nonce has a "ck=" or "ik=".

This happens because the function, while trying to remove the actual CK
and IK parameters, completely ignores other parameters and their quoted
values, and just searches for a "ck=" or "ik=" substring in the complete
header, even within other parameter values.

For detailed info, follow this link:
