The ISC module searches _sequentially_ in all triggers. On a match, the
search is put on hold and the message is forwarded. The filtering
continues when the request comes back from the previous AS.

For how IMS should work, I recommend a book that seems to be quite
popular: Gonzalo Camarillo, Miguel A. Garcia-Martin - "The 3G IP
Multimedia Subsystem (IMS) - Merging the Internet and the Cellular
Worlds" - 2nd Edition - Wiley.
However, please don't come back and ask for features just because they
are there. This is a beginners guide, so also read the relevant
3GPP/TISPAN TS and IETF RFCs.

-Dragos
