Hi,

I defined a trigger on INVITE requests from the user
romain at open-ims.test. Here is the XML defining the trigger :

<TriggerPoint>
	<ConditionTypeCNF>true</ConditionTypeCNF>
	<SPT>
		<ConditionNegated>false</ConditionNegated>
		<Group>0</Group>
		<Method>INVITE</Method>
	</SPT>
	<SPT>
		<ConditionNegated>false</ConditionNegated>
		<Group>1</Group>
		<SIPHeader>
			<Header>From</Header>
			<Content>romain</Content>
		</SIPHeader>
	</SPT>
</TriggerPoint>

There are no other triggers defined in my default service profile, but
when I make a call from marc at open-ims.test to romain at open-ims.test, the
trigger is activated. Here is the trace from scscf.out :

How can the trigger can be activated in this case ?
