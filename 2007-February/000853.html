Hi Babu,

I am not sure what that field with Phone URL does... But it clearly is
not a public identity for the user, as I don't see it in the registrar
as such. The S-CSCF would resolve it if it finds it in the registrar as
a public identity.

Peter/Adrian, could you please tell us what that field represents and
how it should be used? My guess is that you should fill just one?...

In my tests, I have replace sip:alice at open-ims.test with tel:+4930...,
and although it won't make a difference, I used another HSS.

One issue is that SER does not support tel uri in the tm module. But I
don't think that this was your real issue.

-Dragos
