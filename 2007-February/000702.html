Hi all,

I succeeded registering with X-Lite to SIP2IMS gateway. I would like to say
thank you for everyone who gave me a tip to move on. Here is my
configuration.


Register to OSIMS with X-Lite

Setup:

Realm: ims.lab
HSS FQDN: hss.ims.lab
HSS IP: 10.0.3.10
P-CSCF FQDN: pcscf.ims.lab
P-CSCF IP: 10.0.3.6
P-CSCF port: 5060
S-CSCF FQDN: scscf.ims.lab
S-CSCF IP: 10.0.3.8
I-CSCF port: 6060
I-CSCF FQDN: icscf.ims.lab
I-CSCF IP: 10.0.3.7
I-CSCF port: 5060
SIP2IMS GW FQDN: sip2ims.ims.lab
SIP2IMS GW IP: 10.0.3.9

SIP2IMS GW port: 5060

X-Lite IP: 10.0.0.59

Version:
X-Lite: Win32 Version 3.0 build 34025
OSIMS: revision 97

X-Lite configuration:
Display name: tmpa
User name: tmpa
Password: A
Authorization user name: tmpa at ims.lab
Domain: ims.lab

Send outbound via proxy, address: sip2ims.ims.lab

In MySQL database:

sip2ims/credentials table:
auth_username: tmpa (not tmpa at ims.lab, it was one problem for me)
did: _none
realm: ims.lab
password: A
flags: 1
ha1: <irrelevant>
ha1b: 24cb5eddd267a36c287ffe778aeaf39a
uid: 3

ha1b is calculated by:
ser_ims/utils/gen_ha1/gen_ha1 tmpa at ims.lab ims.lab A

hssdb/impi table:
impi_string: tmpa at ims.lab
imsu_id: 3
impi_id: 1
imsi: tmpa_ISDN_User_part_ID
scscf_name: sip:scscf.ims.lab:6060
s_key: 41000000000000000000000000000000
auth_scheme: Digest-AKAv1-MD5
amf: 0000
algorithm: AKAv1
operator_id: 00000000000000000000000000000000
sqn: 000000000000
chrg_id: 1
uiccType: 0
keyLifeTime: 3600

In s_key, 41 stands for 'A', the password set in sip2ims/credentials and in
X-Lite configuration.
