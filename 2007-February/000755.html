Hi all,

I have started looking at tel uris for the CSCFs. So far, after r131, it
is possible to register with a tel uri. I am attaching the SIPp
scenarios that I used for testing.

It seems though, that the tm module lacks support for sending requests
to a tel uri. As such, subscriptions to reg fail now.

I would be interested in what scenario would you like to have with tel
uris... Calls to a tel uri?
