> Hello Josef,
>
> Probably there was a problem while getting the sources. Please look for
> the ser_ims/modules/pcscf/sip.* files. If you don't have them, make
> links to the ones in the scscf module:
> ln -sf ser_ims/modules/scscf/sip.c ser_ims/modules/pcscf/sip.c
> ln -sf ser_ims/modules/scscf/sip.h ser_ims/modules/pcscf/sip.h
>
> You will most likely have the same issues with the icscf module and also
> with some other files there. Normally, SVN should've created the links
> for you.

> -Dragos
