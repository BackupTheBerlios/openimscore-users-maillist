Hi Babu,

I did some more patches and since r138 you can also make a call to a tel
uri if that uri is registered. This solves your scenario. Anything else?

would be interesting to automatically route to the route[PSTN] when not
able to resolve such a tel URI to a sip contact... What do you think?

Also, there is an enum module that could be used to translate a tel uri
into the e164 address.

-Dragos
