I just had a short look into the tracefile (see attached scenario-files).
The trace shows as the first issue that the ACK request of
sip:irsa at open-ims.test (packet 100) is not forwarded by P-CSCF.
When I look into the log-file of P-CSCF which you provided yesterday I can
see that P-CSCF cannot recognize the ACK as being valid. The log-file shows
	DBG:P-CSCF:r_assert_identity: Contact not found
This is just a first hint to the problem. I also had a quick look into the
code:
The function r_assert_identity in module registrar.c calls get_r_contact in
module registrar_storage.c, and this function obviously could not find the
contact of the dialog in its storage.
I don't know why.

Here I stop now due to limited time. I hope that helps for further analysis.
