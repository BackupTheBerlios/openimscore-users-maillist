Hi Thomas,

> I have another question regarding Application Servers. Do they send a
> REGISTER to the S-CSCF with their PSI? I read that ones I have constructed
an
> AS I can add the war-file in the HSS using http://localhost:8080 and
Create
> Service. I was thinking that maybe, instead of sending REGISTER
transactions,
> the AS is registered with its PSI when I upload the war-file?

To be precise: You are uploading the war-file to the AS which - maybe in
your installation - runs on the same server as the HSS.
These two servers (HSS and AS) are totally decoupled, only connected via
iFCs.
An automatic creation of PSIs is therefore not possible.
