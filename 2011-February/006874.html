I looked at the UCT trace and I see the following:

Your platform uses IPv6 addresses. 
But for handling the PUBLISH request the default iFC of OpenIMSCore is still
active and it forwards the PUBLISH request to an AS on address
"sip:127.0.0.1:5065".
I guess the forwarding to the IPv4 address is the cause of the problem. More
details can be probably seen at the S-CSCF console-log.
