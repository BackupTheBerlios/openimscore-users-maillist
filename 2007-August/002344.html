Rob,

 

look into the mdoule documentation of xlog-module.

There you will find the list of implemented specifiers und you will see that
the request URI is printed with %ru.

 

I for example use the following xlog-statement to see the request entering
the x-CSCF:

 

            xlog("L_INFO","%Cyb%cs %rm %ru from %si:%sp From: %fu  To:
%tu\n%Cxx");
