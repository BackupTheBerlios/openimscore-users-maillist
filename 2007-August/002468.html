Hello Franz,

Are you using the latest revision of the HSS? If yes, be aware that the PPR
messages are not sent any more in an automated way, as I considered to be
very inefficient; you should send the PPR manually from the interface.
Regarding the S-CSCF, I know that he updates the user profile only after
a new registration and not after re-registration. If you send the PPR, you
won't need it any more the de-registration & registration procedures.

Cheers,
Adrian
