Hi,

> PROBLEM: shouldnt the S-CSCF of abc.com strip of P asserted ID header???
> Since INVITE is going out of its domain and UE1 has asked it to hide it in
> Privacy header with value given: Id.

That's o.k. Both domains (abc and xyz) are IMS-domains that have a
trust-relationship. Therfore the P-Asserted-Identity header field remains in
the message until it passes the border to an untrusted network-node (UE2). 
The P-CSCF of xyz eliminates the P-Asserted-Identity header field, and
that's correct.
