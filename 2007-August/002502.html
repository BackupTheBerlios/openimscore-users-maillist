Hello Vishwadeepak,

The parameter that you are looking for is this one:
modparam("tm", "fr_timer", 5000)

This indicates how long the S-CSCF waits for ANY (NOT JUST ISC!!!)
transaction to complete until declaring a timeout, 5000ms in this case.
This is why you get 4 messages (at 0, 0.5, 1 and 2 seconds).

The parameters below are not related to this, but to the 3rd party
registration and what is the amount of time with which the registration
towards the app servers would be bigger than the real one.

In the end, I would really not recommend you to reduce the fr_timer to
500 ms as this would mean that !ANY! transaction not finished in this
amount will timeout.

-Dragos
