Yes, it's not necessary to build the libraries separately. They are
included in the projects. The JavaDiameterPeer is a precompiled jar in
FHoSS and the CDiameterPeer compiles as a module in ser_ims.

They are provided separately so that you could easily use them in
different components or new developments. More people using them means
more bugs fixed.

-Dragos
