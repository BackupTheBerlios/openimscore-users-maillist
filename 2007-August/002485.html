Hi Jose!

your UE does not insert the Record-Route headers in its 200 OK... so first I think the ACK should have been sent directly to UE and not to S-CSCF according to SIP-behaviour, the S-CSCF shouldn't have received this message. Second, because of the record-route header, AS should add in its ACK the corresponding Route-headers!

Actually I think the P-CSCF should have rejected the malicious 200 OK or at least have done anything about it...

br, Klemens
