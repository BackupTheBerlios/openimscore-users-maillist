Hi Jeff,

you are getting the 401 because that's the correct behaviour for AKA
authentication with the IMS core (which also the UCT client uses). 

Retry with setting the "registration_default_algorithm" to MD5 in the
S-CSCF and make sure that the IMPI has "all" or at least "Digest-MD5"
set for an authentication scheme in the HSS web console.

Regards,

Peter
