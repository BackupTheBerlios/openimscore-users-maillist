I think there's something with your client...
1. Register (UE->PCSCF), security-client header: port-c=12345, port-s=3062
2. 401 (PCSCF->UE), security-server header: port-c=4060, port-s=4060

so far so good... now the UE should sent an encrypted message from 12345-port to 4060... this is not the case, UE uses the usual ports (i think this is why the response from pcscf is 494).

furthermore:
3. 2nd Register (UE->PCSCF), security-client header: port-c=2468, port-s=7531, security-verify: port-c=8642, port-s=7531
i.e. the 2nd Regisers security-header are completely different from the currently createt SAs... and somehow the ports of the second register remind me on the examples of tech-invite :-/

how did you configure your client (unfortunately i haven't used sipp yet)?
