Hi Adam,

OpenSIPS, just like OpenSER, SER, Kamailio, etc, are at the core the 
same thing. They are all forks of the original SER project, which we 
also use as a base for our CSCFs.

Then the answer to your question is the same as before. It is certainly 
possible to use a SER-based system as an AS and in many cases it was one 
of the best choices, but we would not recommend it as SER was not 
designed or implemented for serving as an AS platform, as understood in 
the NGN standards.

Cheers,
-Dragos
