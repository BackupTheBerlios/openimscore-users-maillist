I guess it is the qop="auth,auth-int".... parameter in the
"WWW-Authenticate" header field, which has been added to OpenIMSCore later
then the mentioned hint. Some clients cannot handle the qop parameter
accordingly.

To disable the qop parameter you have to modify in scscf.cfg the line:
	modparam("scscf","registration_qop","auth,auth-int")
to
	modparam("scscf","registration_qop","")

maybe that helps.
