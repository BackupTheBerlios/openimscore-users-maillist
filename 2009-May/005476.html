Unfortunately I don't have the details of the project with me now, but I
can sum up how I did it.

I've modified the sip client
http://wiki.opensource.nokia.com/projects/SofSipCli
to have it working with OpenIMS; this was not hard, just some headers'
adjusting for the authorization part, recompiling and debugging a few
SIP packets.


Once everything works from the PC, it's also easy to cross-compile the
client and the sofia-sip stack for the maemo platform on Nokia 770
http://www.scratchbox.org/


I'm sorry this information is not much, but I hope it can be of help to
the community. At least I can say this can be done.
