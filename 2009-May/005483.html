Hi Rafik,

> I'm trying to register with X-lite client into my core Openims, but I
> always have the error message "401 - Unauthorized Challenging the UE".

You have to be aware that 3GPP based IMS uses AKAv1-MD5 algorithm for
authentication which X-Lite does not support. You have to configure HSS and
S-CSCF properly. This issue has been addressed often at the mailing-list.

> By the way, the SQN parameter of the FHoSS does increment every
> registration attempt. (could it help).

That's correct. That has to be so.
