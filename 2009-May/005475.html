Hello!

The way the Open IMS Core P-CSCF deals with Security-Client headers does not 
seem to follow the specifications. According to 3GPP TS 33.203, annex H, the 
default value of the parameter "prot" is "esp", but a recent Open IMS Core 
uses "ah" by default. Is this intentional?

The attached patch makes "esp" the default.
