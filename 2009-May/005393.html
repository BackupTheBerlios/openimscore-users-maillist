> From your experience could you please tell me if I can use Asterisk +
> openIMSCore to make Mobile(IMS client) to land calls successfully provided

I suppose it should work.
 
> Do you see any other issues with ASterisk used as pstn gateway ?

Usually a PSTN-gateway is based on an SS7-ISUP link (NNI), but you probably
use an UNI-interface. That's the difference. - And of course all related
standards for SIP/ISUP interworking are not applicable.

But in principle it should work.
