> ... but Asterisk rejects the call
> saying tel:+123 extension not found and also complains about "sip header
> not found"

Asterisk obviously does not support TEL-URIs.
It does not behave as a 3GPP conforming a PSTN-gateway. That should not be a
surprise.
  
> When i try dialing sip:+123 at open-ims.test.user=phone from UCT IMS client,
> openIMSCore routes it to asterisk and the call gets established with
> asterisk.

> Could anyone pls sugggest if any conversion needs to be done when the
> called number contains tel uri --> tel:+123 at openIMSCore or Asterisk ??

According to your experience, such a conversion should be done.
When you ask where it should be done the answer is: outside of OpenIMSCore,
because OpenIMSCore follows the standards.
When you ask where it is most easy to be done the answer is: in OpenIMSCore.

I hope that helps.
