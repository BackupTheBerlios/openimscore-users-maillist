Michael,

> can somebody help me to resolve the problem?

Various authentication methods (for different access networks) have been
defined for IMS and are also implemented in OpenIMS. According to 3GPP only
IMS AKA (TS 33.203 $6.1) has to be used in 3GPP networks, which is the
default method provisioned in OpenIMSCore (Digest-AKAv1-MD5). This method is
obviously not supported by Eyebeam.

But there are other methods available in OpenIMSCore. Look at the HSS GUI at
Private User Identities - Authentication schemes. For Eyebeam you may select
Digest-MD5. In parallel you also have to change the
"registration_default_algorithm" parameter in scscf.cfg to "HSS-Selected".
	modparam("scscf","registration_default_algorithm","HSS-Selected")

I hope that works for you.
Just an additional info: The "Authorization user name" parameter in Eyebeam
has to be in full NAI format (Network Attachment Identifier) which includes
the realm like "alice at net1.test".
