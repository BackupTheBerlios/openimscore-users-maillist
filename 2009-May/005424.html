> the problem is that when i close my PC the processes go down, or at
> least I don't know how to reattach to their output later.

In this case I use "screen" and reattach with "screen -d -r". This works
perfect.
 
> By default is all the logging just in foreground or is it already
> redirected to some files? I could not find them.

Default is just in the foreground. But you can redirect to specific files
using a "log_facility" directive in the xcscf.cfg-files and using syslog.
