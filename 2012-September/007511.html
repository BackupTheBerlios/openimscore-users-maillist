Hi all,

I am trying to do a test with the IMS as below -

(1) UE ----------------------REGISTRATION------------------>IMS Core
                                           (with a short expiration time = 30 seconds)

 (2)     <---------------------401 challenge---------------------
  (3)    --------------REGISTRATION with credentials------->
  (4)    <---------------------200 OK -----------------------------


The UE will re-register after half the time has expired, i.e., after t=15 seconds. Assume, the UE has also subscribed to its own registration state.
 
     UE ----------------------SUBSCRIBE------------------>IMS Core
           <---------------------200 OK -----------------------


Is there an option now in the IMS core for us to choose whether the UE has to re-authenticate or not as a result of re-registration due to a refresh?

i.e.,

case a)  After t=15,

(5) UE ----------------------REGISTRATION------------------>IMS Core
(6)     <---------------------401 challenge---------------------
(7)     --------------REGISTRATION with credentials------->
(8)      <---------------------200 OK -----------------------------


OR

case b)  After t=15,

(5) UE ----------------------REGISTRATION------------------>IMS Core
(8)       <---------------------200 OK -----------------------------


Is case b) configurable in the IMS core ? i.e.,  avoiding step 6 and 7 ? (avoiding a re-authentication because of a registration refresh)

Also, Is it possible to send a NOTIFY (from the IMS core) to let the UE know what the IMS core  intends to do   - i.e., whether to challenge a re-registration( i.e., Re-authenticate) or not.


Thanks,
Badri.
