why don't you just use a Shell-Script and write it to the database
directly? That should not be too hard, it's basically just 5 tables:
- IMSU (IMS SUbscription)
- IMPI (Private identities)
- IMPU (Public identities)
- IMPU_VISITED_NETWORK (allowed visited networks for an IMPU)
- IMPI_IMPU (Private/Public Identitiy mapping)
