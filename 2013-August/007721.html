Hi Michel,

 

obviously you sent the INVITE to P-CSCF. The P-CSCF is obliged to check if
each request comes from an eligible subscriber (which has registered
before).

Just send the request to the I-CSCF (port 5060) and the behavior will be
different.

 

BR Franz
