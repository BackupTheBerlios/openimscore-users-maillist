Hi Majdi.

Yes, the scenario is correct, but if you want to use a SIP client (like
X-Lite), you have to take care with authentification model. Configure
your users to use Digest-MD5. This is to let you doing REGISTER. Then if
you want to INVITE between users, you have to enforce the service routes
in p-cscf (look into pcscf.cfg and use "variant 2" inside
route[Orig_Initial] function).

In the next thread of mailing list you can found tips to configure
X-Lite:
http://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/openimscore-users/2007-September/004137html

Regards...
