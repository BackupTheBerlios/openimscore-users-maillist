Hi Majdi.

You have to play with configuration files.
A tip: execute the script /opt/OpenIMSCore/ser_ims/cfg/configurator.sh
and observe what files it touch. That should be more or less the files
that you will have to modify.

Also you will have to touch the open-ims dns
zone: /etc/bind/open-ims.dnszone

And search old messages in this list. This topic is recurrent.
