2008/3/5, Christian Esteve <chesteve at gmail.com>:
>
> You should point to the S-CSCF, consider the PSTN gateway acting as a
> Border and Media Gateway Control Function (BGCF and MGCF as per ETSI
> TISPAN) at the originating domain and your S-CSCF + P-CSCF the
> terminating domain.
>
> If you consider the PSTN to be in your own domain you will need to
> "register" your PSTN GW acting as an "Access Border Gateway Function".
>
> Hope it helps, had no practical experience with PSTN interconnection.
