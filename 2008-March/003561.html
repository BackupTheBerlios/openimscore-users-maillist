Hi,

While testing our application in the openimscore we encountered some
problems with processing of the CANCEL request.

The scenario is the following UE1 makes a call to UE2.
During the call there is an interaction with AS.
scscf forwards INVITE to AS and AS changes the Request-URI (to UE3) and
passes the
request back to the scscf.
Scscf then forwards the request according to the changed destination.
Problem arises when UE1 generates CANCEL request after receiving of the '180
ringing response' from UE3.
Strange is that when UE3 receives CANCEL it generates '481 Call
leg/transaction does not exists' response.
And just after this one another '486 Busy here'.

I attached the network trace from wireshark.

Please point us what could be wrong.
