>Hello all,

>Just a pair of questions:

>What is the difference between UCTPDF and PoCCA?

UCTPDF is made in java by the people of the University of Cape Town and well, just look at the source code..

PoCCA is written in C by us from Fraunhofer FOKUS (they same people that did the OpenIMSCore,
its made of the P-CSCF (Open Source in the Branch), [that you probably also use with UCTPDF]
the PCRF and a trial PCEF to be used in a Linux Router (if you dont have a GGSN...). 
It has the standard interfaces based on 3GPP Release 7, with all the mandatory
functionality and some of the optional.

The functionality it offers is basicly 
Call Admission Control (base on linux firewall) ,Diffserv packet marking, Resource usage monitoring and the events in 
the PCEF (detects loss of bearer and user exceeding bandwidth limits , reacts upon policies) .. 

We demonstrated the usage of PoCCA in the IMS Workshop in November

I dont have the figures of a real comparision between them but it can be foreseen that PoCCA supports a bit more load than UCTPDF 
(i have tested PCRF on 100CPS but i haven´t done a real benchmark, whenever its done it will be in the website 
http://www.fokus.fraunhofer.de/bereichsseiten/testbeds/ims_playground/components/pocca.php?lang=en
)

Also for what I read in some papers of the people of UCT they don´t follow specifications, deffinitely they dont follow the 
Diameter Auth State machine (its not implemented for the Java Diameter Peer and they havent done it)
