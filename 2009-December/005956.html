You're welcome.
As far as your registration problem with Open IMS Core (using Symbian
client), I am pretty sure that your main problem is an improper
authentification algorithm. If I am not wrong, Open IMS Core does not
support all available authentification algorithms and your client was likely
configured to use one which was not supported. I'm guessing that the OpenSBC
"solved" your problem only in that that it did registration on behalf of
your device, with proper selection of authentification algorithm etc.
Therefore if you were to configure your device to use one of the supported
OpenIMS Core authentification algs I am sure you would succesfully register
without a proxy. The best way to verify if this is correct would be to post
a  wireshark trace with  your "Setting 2" scenario.
