Dear all,

I would like to inform you that deadline for submission of papers for 
ONIT 2010 - 2nd Open NGN and IMS Testbeds Workshop @ TRIDENTCOM 2010 
*has been extended until 17.12.2009*.

As the targets of this workshop are spot-on for this community, we are 
encouraging you to submit your work on NGN/IMS Testbeds and then 
hopefully join us on May 18th in Berlin to friendly and hopefully very 
productive discussions.

For more detailed information visit *http://www.onit-ws.org/*.

Please feel free to forward this information and attached CfP to your 
colleagues. The organizing committee and TPC members will appreciate if 
you can promote this event across community.

Best Regards,
Dragos Vingarzan
