Hello,

Unfortunatly, the Mercuro IMS Client project has been stopped an the team
has been disolved. The technical assets are frozen and are no longer usable.

Mamadou Diop (one of the technical project manager) has started two new
projects (completly unreleated to Mercuro): Doubango (
http://www.doubango.org/) and IMSDroid (http://code.google.com/p/imsdroid/);
their focus are mainly the mobile platforms (iPhone and Android).

There are also some plans to build a new PC clients (Windows, MacOS X,
Linux), but it may take some time due to the complexity.

Regards, Laurent Etiemble.
