Hi Veena,

First, have you got a presence server in your network? FYI: there is no PS
included in OpenIMS...

Then, you should investigate the needs/pre-requisities of the Presence
service to provision it in your core network. You find this information in
3GPP TS 24.141 (http://www.3gpp.org/ftp/Specs/html-info/24141.htm). See
Annex A.3 "Signalling flows demonstrating how watchers subscribe to presence
event notification" for a detailed, commented example. Pay special attention
to the parts mentioning "initial Filter Criteria" (for SUBSCRIBE and PUBLISH
requests).

When you know, or believe you know, the pre-requisities follow the
guidelines in http://openimscore.org/faq/46 to provision the service with
OpenIMS. See 3GPP TS 29.228
(http://www.3gpp.org/ftp/specs/html-info/29228.htm), and in particular Annex
B for more information about how iFC relates to service profiles etc.

Best regards,
Andreas
