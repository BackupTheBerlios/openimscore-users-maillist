Hi,
> When I use DNF in the trigger point configuration, I see REGISTER AND
> SUBSCRIBE in SPT section on the Web Console. And when I use CNF, it
> shows as REGISTER OR SUBSCRIBE. So, which one is correct ? CNF should be
> OR operation or AND operation?

According to TS 29.228:

A Trigger Point expression is constructed out of atomic expressions (i.e.
Service Point Trigger) linked by Boolean operators AND, OR and NOT. Any
logical expression constructed in that way can be transformed to forms
called Conjunctive Normal Form (CNF) and Disjunctive Normal Form (DNF).

A Boolean expression is said to be in Conjunctive Normal Form if it is
expressed as a conjunction of disjunctions of literals (positive or
negative atoms), i.e. as an AND of clauses, each of which is the OR of one
of more atomic expressions.

Further details you can find in Annex C of above mentioned document.
