In short:
- small patches - you can send them directly to the mailing lists and
we'll pick them up and commit in the trunk
- bigger ones - sign-up on berlios.de and tell us your username and a
very brief description of what you want to do and we'll open up a branch
for you. When you are done and satisfied with how it works, we'll help
you merge that into the trunk. During development, you'll also be able
to enjoy help from the rest of the OpenIMSCore community.

We'd like to run a Wiki-style SVN, so any contributions are welcomed and
there's no need to worry about commit rights.

Cheers,
-Dragos
