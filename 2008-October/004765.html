Hi Miroslave,

basically you would have three options to store that info from the VoD
AS:
a) use the transparent repository data offered via Sh to store in the
HSS
b) dump the information via XCAP to a XDMS
c) store it locally with the AS

As I always see the HSS more to hold static user profile information, I
tend to prefer the XDMS option as this one often holds the service
related details. In case you need to make that info available also to
other instances, either a) or b) will work just fine.

AFAIK also the IPTV specs of ETSI, OpenIPTVForum and alike are not very
clear about this point ...

Regards,

Peter
