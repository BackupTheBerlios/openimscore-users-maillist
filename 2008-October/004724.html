Just send me result of "svn diff" and I will review/try/commit ASAP to
trunk. If you need to do more tests or are unsure, I'll just open a
branch for you and you can commit there yourself right-away.

For trunk write access we would prefer to give that away only to
developers that already have branches in development. Also we'd like to
limit the bigger trunk commits for each project to just a few people
that have the big picture, in order to keep a relatively stable trunk,
because we don't have other stable releases.
