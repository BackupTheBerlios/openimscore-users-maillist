Hello,

Regarding the Mercuro IMS Client, IPv6 is available starting with the
Silver Edition (P.U. EUR 79.00). The freely available edition (the
Bronze Edition) does not allow IPv6.

Note that Mercuro works fine on IPv6 with OpenIMSCore, so if you have
further questions, then go ahead.

Regards, Laurent Etiemble.

Need an IMS Client ? Why don't you try out Mercuro IMS Client.
The Mercuro IMS Client is fully SIP/3GPP/IMS compliant.
More information at http://www.mercuro.net/
