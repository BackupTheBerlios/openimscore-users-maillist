Hi Rafik,

> In the core openims, useally an IMPI can have many IMPU, but could the
> IMPU have many IMPI, then what about the IMSU?

The IMSU (IMS Subscription) corresponds to the legal contract between a
subscriber and an operator, irrespective how many IMPIs and IMPUs will be
sassigned to the subscriber.

Multiple IMPIs of a subscriber may share some IMPUs. This leads to the
classical SIP forking situation.

> How could a Presence Server differentiate between two IMPI corresponding
> to one IMPU ?

There is an 'id' attribute for each tuple. See RFC 3863:
The <tuple> element MUST contain an 'id' attribute which is used to
distinguish this tuple from other tuples in the same PRESENTITY.
