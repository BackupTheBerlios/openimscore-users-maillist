> However, when the ACK is sent from a6 -> a1 (after receiving a 200 ok) ,
it
> goes through 4 proxies namely, mo.pcscf at open-ims.test, mo.scscf at open-

> 
> The terminating proxy i.e., mt.pcscf at open-ims.test should have sent it to

> earlier correctly!
> But this alone is not happening. Am I missing anything here? From DNS

There are 2 issues with your client:
1. It does not include a Contact header field in 200 OK of INVITE.
2.  The ACK does not take the Contact address as target address (it is not
available because of above issue).
