After rethinking about your issue it became clear to me.
The two URIs <sip:7778889999 at lab.com.ar> and
<sip:7778889999 at lab.com.ar;user=phone> are different ones.
In the first case the userpart is treated as a string but in the second case
it is interpreted as a telefone number. 

Stripping off the parameter "user=phone" is therefore not allowed because it
would mean to change the address.

The only way to solve your problem is therfore to bring your UE to omit the
user=phone parameter. Then the subscription will work.
