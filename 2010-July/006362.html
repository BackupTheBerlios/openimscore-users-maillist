Hi Tran,

> I'm interested at Policy Control QoS in OpenIMSCore. I know that a project
> is UCT Policy Control Framework to integrate into OpenIMSCore, but now
> this project is not available.
> What is tool to support Policy Control QoS  in OpenIMSCore ?
> Or Does OpenIMSCore supports Policy Control QoS ?

The OpenIMSCore supports the Rx interface. This is all what is necessary
from IMS to support Policy Control and QoS.
You now need the other side of the interface: the PCRF and the other parts
of PCC (see 3GPP TS 23.203).
