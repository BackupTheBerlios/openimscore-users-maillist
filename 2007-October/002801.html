Hi,
 
In addition to Mobicents there is also "Project Sailfin"
(http://sailfin.dev.java.net/). Some Sailfin-examples are available at
http://wiki.glassfish.java.net/Wiki.jsp?page=SipExamples.
 
The major difference between these to is that Mobicents implements JAIN SLEE
whereas "Project Sailfin" implements the SIP Servlet specification.
