Hi,
 
Provisioning is mainly performed through the FHoSS Management console.
 
Under "Services" you'll need to:
 
1) Create an "Application server" which points to your computer running
Mobicents.
 
2) Define a Trigger point for your service. See info about the service to
figure out what shall be triggered.
 
3) Create an Initial Filter Criteria which includes your Application Server
and Trigger Point.
 
4) Create a Service Profile based on your IFC created above.
 
Then, use this Service Profile in one of your user identities.
 
See also books on IMS, such as "The 3G IP Multimedia Subsystem - Merging the
internet and the cellular worlds" by Camarillo and Garcia-Martin.
 
Hope this helps!
 
BR,
Andreas Häber
