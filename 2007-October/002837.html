Hi Daniel,

man, this made me scratch my head so hard that now it hurts :-p. Please,
next time, send a full trace that also shows the messaging inside the
core - capture on all interfaces on the core machine.

So, your re-INVITE is not correct. According to my SIP knowledge, your
Request-URI should be the dialog target, as in the Contact address that
you got in the 200 OK. I couldn't figure out the strange scenario that
you are doing such that you get 2x200OK for the first INVITE, but choose
one contact from there ;-). It seems that the P-CSCF gets the INVITE on
the terminating side and although it should identify it right as
terminating subsequent and then make a nasty loop, it checks first if
the S-CSCF sending it was registered, which is dumb, so a 403 saves the day.

Cheers,
-Dragos
