Hello Didier,

in packet 45 and later the INVITE from the S-CSCF is sent to
127.0.0.1:4060. According to your DNS, this is probably
pcscf.open-ims.test. However, you have moved the P-CSCF from 127.0.0.1.

So most probably you need to update your DNS.
