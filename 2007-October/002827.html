Hi Pedro,

 

Look in pcscf.cfg. That is where the service routes are checked and
where the 400 response is generated. At each place where this is checked
in that file (just search for "indicated Service-Routes" for example),
there is another variant that is commented out by default. Just
uncomment the other variant and comment the original one. It basically
enforces correct service routes instead of rejecting the request,

 

This should take care of that problem.
