Hi Aleem,
 
> I want to make PC-PSTN call and vice-versa.Can some one tell me all the
> required components which should be up and running for doing so?

You need a SIP-PSTN gateway. Asterisk or SEMS can be used for that.

> For installation I followed the steps given on
"http://openimscore.org".But
> when I am downloading the components from
> http://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/openimscore/ser_ims/trunk , the
> bgcf.cfg,bgcf.xml and bgcf.sh are not available which are required for PC-
> PSTN call.

There is no BGCF component included in OpenIMSCore, but you have PSTN
handles inside of scscf.cfg, wher you can route calls to a SIP-PSTN gateway.
