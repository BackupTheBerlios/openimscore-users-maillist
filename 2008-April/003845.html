Hi Arnaud,

It's a matter of who connects first to who. In your case, it was the HSS 
that did connect back to the I and S. Obviously, as we run all on the 
same machines, there's a shortage of 3868 ports ;-). So try and do what 
Enrique suggested, but for ports 3869 and 3870. Then wireshark will also 
decode those ports as Diameter.

Another trick is to start the HSS first...

Cheers,
-Dragos
