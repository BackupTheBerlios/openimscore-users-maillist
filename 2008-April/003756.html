Hi Beatriz,

I am Alberto Diez from Fraunhofer FOKUS, I am the person who wrote the 
README you refer to in your previous email.
The instructions are to run the branch version of the P-CSCF, and is not 
part of the trunk project so thats why its not in the VMware image 
distributed.
Just download it and install it normaly.

After which you can probably use it. We did a big effort to comply with 
standards but of course many optional things are missing. You are 
welcome to contribute to the project.

We also have a PCRF and PCEF that are not Open Source, you can read 
about them in
http://www.fokus.fraunhofer.de/bereichsseiten/testbeds/ims_playground/components/pocca.php?lang=en
