The Request-URI and Route headers are wrong. Request-URI should be 
remote Contact and Route header should have the dialog's Recorded-Route 
values, as seen in packet 3. Seems that you added the Contact to the 
Route list at the bottom. Indeed, it is processed last, but then the 
Request-URI is wrong, which Request-URI would be actually processed 
after all Routes have been consumed.

Please refer to the RFC3261.
