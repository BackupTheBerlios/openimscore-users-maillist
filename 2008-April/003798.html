Hi Marc,

That's the way i use:


String callee = "<sip:07939830498 at domain.com">;

SipFactory sipFactory = (SipFactory)
getServletContext().getAttribute(SIP_FACTORY);

URI calleeURI = sipFactory.createURI(callee);

req.setRequestURI(calleeURI);


where req is the SipServletRequest (an INVITE request here).

Remember that according to the RFC you can only change the request-uri,
not the "to" field.

I hope it helps.

Regards, Enrique
