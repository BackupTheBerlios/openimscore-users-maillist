Hi Enrique,

we only support two wildcards  * and ? , i have to recognise we do not 
support what the specification says , so if you have the time to 
contribute to the code with the real 3gpp wildcards defined in the TS 
for numbering and addressing would be great.

So you could define ?@open-ims.test  but that would also send to your AS 
the x y z and any other character not only the 1 2 3 as it seems you want

I would say you change your id scheme.  and then put in the space for 
wildcard psi the wildcarded expression  ?@open-ims.test   and that will 
match..

and just follow the guideline i gave in a previous post.


There are some bugs in the implementation of the PSI but right now I do 
not have time to look at them. If you have the time, again your 
contribution will be appreciated.


good luck!
