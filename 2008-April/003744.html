Found out the issue by looking in ser_ims/cfg/cfg.lex. The "no" is 
reserved as a keyword. ims-hio.yes doesn't work either ;-). The solution 
is to quote you name in the alias field: alias="ims-hio.no"

Cheers,
-Dragos
