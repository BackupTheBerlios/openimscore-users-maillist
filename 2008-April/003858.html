>  Of course, sorry. Here you have the capture on the wireshark. 

I have now analyzed the trace with the following result:

The terminating INVITE is received by the P-CSCF but not recognized as such.
Usually the terminating INVITE is handled by route[Term_Initial] in
pcscf.cfg.

I do not understand why the INVITE is not handled by one of the "route[]"
procedures of pcscf.cfg. All procedures contain a log statement which
produces a specific console-log like ">>       Term_Initial". But in your
case there is no such a log statement. It looks like the INVITE is silently
absorbed.

I recommend that you set further trace-points in pcscf.cfg to find what
happens to the terminating INVITE.
