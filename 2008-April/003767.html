Hi Tomasz,

> In RFC
> 3327 when UA receives response for REGISTER request it takes the contents
> of the Path header set to construct the correct Route header set.

No, that's not the case. Check §5.1 of RFC 3327 again. 

The Path header field is used by the S-CSCF as a route inserted at
terminating requests, and the Service-Route header field is used by the UA
as a route inserted at originating requests.
