> ... Going through the RFC, it says that the Service-Route is not
> compulsory to be followed - is this specific for Open Ims Core only?

In 3GPP specs it is mandatory to follow the Service-Route. If the terminal
does not insert the correct Service-Route based Route header field the
P-CSCF will reject the request or overwrite with the correct one.

In RFC 3608 the Service-Route header is an optional mechanism but in 3GPP
architecture it is enforced.
