The simplest would probably be to have an initial Filter Criteria 
trigger on the calls and forward them to the voicemail server. Then the 
voicemail server could act as a B2BUA and detect if the user does not 
answer in a given time or if it answers with a 486. Then it can answer 
itself the call and record the message.

Other than provisioning of the service in the HSS, I don't see what 
you'd need to change in the core.
