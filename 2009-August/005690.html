Indeed. The P-CSCF implements the NAT mechanisms also to avoid 
situations like this. You are registering the 10.x.x.x contact and then 
try to send an INVITE from 192.168.x.x. The P-CSCF rejects the call 
because only the registered contact for that identity is allowed to 
originate signaling with that originating identity.
