Hi Baye,

we are not either familiar with opensips... Sorry, but there are soooo
many flavors of the SIP Express Router and I guess opensips is also one,
that we can't really know all.

So I guess it will be better to ask on the opensips mailing list then
about opensips...

BTW: Open IMS Core has been using the SER from iptel.org for a long time
now. We are in the process of including support also for the new merged
SER+OpenSER/Kamailio, aka sip-router.org. But for opensips, absolutely
no plans. This of course only means that you can't run the Open IMS Core
CSCFs on top of opensips, but you can always inter-operate with any SIP
servers or proxies like opensips.

Cheers,
