Hi Arjun,

You will have to install the Bind server only on one machine, dedicated or
not, as you want.

The only thing you have to do is to change the IP addresses in the dns conf.

Here is an exemple. I have 5 machines :
192.168.1.2 is the DNS.
192.168.1.3 is the P-CSCF
192.168.1.4 is the S-CSCF
192.168.1.5 is the I-CSCF
192.168.1.6 is the HSS

By default, the open-ims.dnszone is like this :
...

ns                      1D IN A         127.0.0.1

pcscf                   1D IN A         127.0.0.1
icscf                   1D IN A         127.0.0.1
scscf                   1D IN A         127.0.0.1
hss                     1D IN A         127.0.0.1


You have to change it to :

ns                      1D IN A         192.168.1.2
pcscf                   1D IN A         192.168.1.3
icscf                   1D IN A         192.168.1.5
scscf                   1D IN A         192.168.1.4

hss                     1D IN A         192.168.1.6


And then restart bind.

Your machines have to ask your DNS for name resolution. Be sure that this
information is in /etc/resolv.conf.

Enjoy.
Arnaud.
