Hi Tomasz,

I might be wrong, but.... Only the clients use P-CSCFs as outbound
proxies, not the AS. If you make origination or termination from the AS,
then you should not send it through an P-CSCF.

The PSI's are not 100% supported right now, but they partially work. For
full support, watch the mailing list as commits and announcements will
soon follow it. There's a patch coming (hope I did not spoiled it ;-) ).

Cheers,
-Dragos
