Hi Jean-Yves,

To mount your shares you first need a mounting point:

mkdir /mnt/hgfs

then to mount all your vmware shared folders use:

mount -t vmhgfs .host:/ /mnt/hgfs

Or you could really mount any smb share over the network, as well...

Cheers,
-Dragos
