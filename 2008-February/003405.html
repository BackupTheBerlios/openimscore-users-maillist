You have to run startup.sh from your /opt/OpenIMSCore/FHoSS/trunk/deploy!

The 2nd line says: "Classpath is lib/*.jar:.:log4j.properties:.." which
means that it tries to load the jars from /opt/OpenIMSCore/lib/*.jar,
which is obviously wrong.

just do:

cd /opt/OpenIMSCore/FHoSS/trunk/deploy
./startup.sh

-Dragos
