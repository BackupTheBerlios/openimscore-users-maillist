Hi Sabyasachi,

There is a problem in the client. Look at packets 49 and 50 in your
trace. The via headers should be identical, yet they are not. They look
like coming from the initial INVITE transaction and not from the PRACK
one.So you have actually answered to the INVITE with a 200 OK that has a
misleading CSeq that looks like the 200 OK is for the PRACK. Well, it is
not, hence the retransmissions. Also the RAck/RSeq headers (look at
packets 44,45) seems quite off...

For the 2nd issue I don't know what to tell you... looks like there was
an S-CSCF restart between 159 and 183...

-Dragos
