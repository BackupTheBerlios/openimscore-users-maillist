Hi Devdutt,

> Attached are the ethereal traces from Xlite and IMS communicator.

Tracefiles from the whole platform including the internal SIP and diameter
messages between x-CSCF would be much better than only a trace from the
edge. See http://www.openimscore.org/installation_guide#step8 

After a quick look at the traces I can guess:

IMS-Communicator:
The credentials are not o.k. This can be verified at the pcscf.log.
BTW you are using an old release of IMS communicator.

X-Lite:
You did not adapt the authentication method (downgrade from AKAv1-MD5 to
MD5). X-Lite does not support AKAv1-MD5.
