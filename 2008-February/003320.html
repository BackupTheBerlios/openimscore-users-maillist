Hi Jin,

> I found that SIPp seemed to register successfully without any
> challenge and response procedure (200 OK SAR succesful in the trace). Do
> you have any clue why this happened? 

The REGISTER forwarded by P-CSCF to I-CSCF (packet 52) already contains
"integrity-protected="yes"" in the Authorization header field. This means a
secure association has already been established (before) and therefore no
further authentication and encryption efforts are necessary. This is usual
in case of a re-registration.
