Hi Viral,
 
> Please do respond with what could be the issue.

I assume that the issue is caused by your domain-name
"lablinux-1165.qualcomm.com" which consists of three parts while the
preconfigured domain "open-ims.test" has only two parts. Therefore some of
the regular expressions (despite you have used "configurator.sh") do not
match. 

Please look at line 166 in scscf.sh which originally looks like
   if (uri=~"sip:(.*)@open-ims\.test(.*)" || uri=~"tel:.*"){

This regular expression does not match anymore in your case I assume.

You have either to adapt all regular expressions accordingly or only use a
domain name consisting of two parts. The "configurator.sh" script does not
work for that kind of change.
