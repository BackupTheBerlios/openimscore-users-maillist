Hi Jean-Yves,

concerning mounting a usbkey on the Linux image

try "dmesg" to check how the disk was recognised e.g sda1

create usbkey directory: "mkdir /media/usbkey"

and then do "mount /dev/sda1 /media/usbkey"
or try "mount -t usbfs /dev/sda /media/usbkey"



concerning the fhoss web interface - it should be available by doing:

http://fhoss_ip_addr:8080 in your browser
