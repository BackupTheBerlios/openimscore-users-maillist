Hi Victoria,

there are two problems:

> ./icscf.sh: line 3: killser: command not found

There are two scripts (killser and stopser) in /opt/OpenIMSCore/ser_ims/cfg/
which your system could not find due to your PATH. Copy the scripts
eventually to /usr/bin and the first error should be gone.

> 0(9473) ERROR: load_module: could not open module
> </opt/OpenIMSCore/ser_ims/modules/mysql/mysql.so>:
> /opt/OpenIMSCore/ser_ims/modules/mysql/mysql.so: undefined symbol: floor

This shows obviously some problems that might have happened during
compilation of mysql. Did you see any hints there?
