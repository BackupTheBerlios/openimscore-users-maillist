Well the CSCF of the OpenIMSCore are based in SER Sip Express Router,
you can find information on SER in http://www.iptel.org/ser  and also
some mailing lists there where you can ask why they decided to use
scripts to describe the behavior of the SIP server, if you this doesnt
let you sleep well

The scripts only describe behavior and its very convenient that way
because its very easy to understand and configure it for many different
purposes and  if you change a small thing in the behavior you just don't
have to recompile only edit the script..

The actual logic is in the code of SER , and the functionalities of
OpenIMSCore are modules that export functions that can be used in the
configuration script of ser...

But it works fine like this , i dont really understand why you ask this...
