Currently we have no plan of implementing in the Open IMS Core.

FYI and not as publicity: FOKUS has a closed-source implementation of
SigComp and iptelorg.com has integrated it into SER. But for the
OpenIMSCore there are not enough resources to motivate the complex
functionality.
