Hi Davide,

You register to your domain. That is snodo. The Request-URI is probably
sip:snodo, which is correct.

Then, the DNS for your home domain should point towards your I-CSCF
because the I-CSCF is the entry point in the network. So change your DNS
and point snodo towards icscf.snodo or directly to .91.

-Dragos
