Hi Tomasz,

 

simply put: distinct is a PSI when there is no room for interpretation
(e.g. sip:chat-ims at open-ims-test) and wildcarded if there is room (e.g.
sip:chat-!.*!@open-ims.test to match for "chat-all" or "chat-101" etc.)

Details in  23.002 

 

Keep looking for updates in the coming weeks for PSI configuration and
support.

I might be wrong, but.... Only the clients use P-CSCFs as outbound
