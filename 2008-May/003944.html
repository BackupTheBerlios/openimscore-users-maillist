Here is my finding:

The iFC of Alice is not correct. You cannot use wildcards if you trigger on
a request-URI like "sip:iptv.*". If you want to do that you have to use a
PSI, which I would recommend in this case.

The result is, that the trigger does not match because the S-CSCF does not
resolve wildcard-URIs if you do not use a PSI. Therefore the S-CSCF tries to
forward the request, but it cannot resolve the URI. You can see the error in
scscf.log line 200 (ERROR: sip_hostport2su: could not resolve hostname:
"iptv_test").
