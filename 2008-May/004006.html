Hi, Depends on the event you want to subscribe to. Seems like youneed to provision Public Service Identities (PSI) to your services (e.g., sip:as1 at example.com and sip:as2 at example.com). In case you haven’tdone this before it is almost the same as creating an IMPU, except you need to changethe “IMPU Type” to Distinct_PSI or Wildcarded_PSI (I think you needthe former in this scenario).
