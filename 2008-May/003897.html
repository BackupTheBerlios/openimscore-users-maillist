If you disable the NAT functionality in the P-CSCF, then it should send 
it there. However, you will have bigger issues as you are only allowed 
to send messages from contact addresses that are already registered 
(this could also be "fixed" by changing the p-cscf file, but it will be 
hard and not advisable).
