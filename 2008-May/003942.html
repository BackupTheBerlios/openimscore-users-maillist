> "ims_cscf_server.trace" does not really cover the the whole IMS
> Core? What do you mean? I captured all the UDP packets, how do I do to
> meet your requirement if not?

In case your OpenIMSCore is running on a single machine (P-, I- and S-CSCF
on one machine) it is sufficient if you create a trace with tcpdump as
follows:

     tcpdump -i any -s 3000 -w /var/tmp/coretrace.pcap not port 22 and not
port 3306

This command helps also in case of packet-fragmentation and selects all
packets (including fragments) without SSH- and MySQL-packets.
