> I didn't get the Notify message after I got the "200OK" message when
> unsubscribing the "reg" event followed by de-register, what is wrong?

Perhaps it is o.k.

Looking into RFC 3265 (3.1.6.2. Confirmation of Subscription
Creation/Refreshing) I only see that sending a NOTIFY is required as
follows: 
"Upon successfully accepting or refreshing a subscription, notifiers MUST
send a NOTIFY message immediately to communicate the current resource state
to the subscriber."

Unsubscribing is neither accepting nor refreshing.
