> Hi pedro antonio,

> There is a new version of the 08_cdp_sessions branch that includes a
> P-CSCF that deals better with the originating and terminating issues.
> With the one before you could generate AAR in the originating and
> terminating side but depending on which sent the BYE there would be an
> issue (it was possible that the session for the terminating side was
> dropped by the originating and the other way around)
>
> Nevertheless, it is already solved, only have to consider that the
> P_generates_AAR function now takes a parameter 0 or 1 depending if
> originating or terminating , and there is a new config variable to state
> where do you want reservation of resources to happen  if you set it to 2
> than its in both sides.
>
> just re-read the README.PCC
>

>
