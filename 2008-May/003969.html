Hi Rémi,

To solve your problem you can use PSI or Wildcarded PSI, as i do. I need
my AS to recieved four different SIP INVITE requests depending on some
conditions.

I have a PSI and four IMPU's and IMPI's whose IFC's are pointing to my
PSI (with no trigger point, cause i want all the requests to be
redirected to my PSI).

This way you can redirect the requests to your AS with different
Request_URI's, and them your AS will act different ways depending on the
request URI.

you can also use Wilcarded PSI instead of four different users, like:

remi.?@open-ims.test

where ? is = my_information


I hope it's useful...

Regards, Enrique.
