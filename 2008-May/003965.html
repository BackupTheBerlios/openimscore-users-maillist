> SCTP - not yet. Would accept contributions to CDiameterPeer and 
> JavaDiameterPeer (if SCTP would work in Java), of course.
>  
> IPv6 - no immediate plans, but it should not be a big deal (SER works). 
> We know that the CDiameterPeer and JavaDiameterPeer might need some 
> slight adjustments, but we never actually tried IPv6. It's always that 
> everybody asks about it, but nobody actually uses it, so it did not make 
> sense to invest time into it.
>  
> -Dragos
