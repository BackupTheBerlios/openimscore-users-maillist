Hi Santosh (must be a common name ;-) ),

The CDiameterPeer and JDiameterPeer are already integrated into ser_ims 
and FHoSS, so don't worry about that.

They are also offered separately so that one could use them for other 
components besides our targets, without going through the trouble of 
extracting them. They might not be exhaustive implementations of the 
Diameter protocol, yet they have been tested for some time already and 
should be quite OK for IMS usage.

Cheers,
-Dragos
