Hi,

I don't understand what you mean by "total integration". If you make that
part clear it might be easier to help you here.

I'm also confused about what you mean by "Neither C2D or CallSetup examples
receive any info on registered users". Do you mean that you would like to
receive REGISTER-requests from S-CSCF for users? If "something else" then
please elaborate. Otherwise, to make a service receive such information it
must be configured correctly first, also notice that "REGISTER is a little
special. See 3GPP TS 23.218 section 6.3 for the details. It will point you
to section 5.2 which gives further insight into this.

If you've configured triggers etc. to receive REGISTER-requests check logs &
traces to see where the problem is... perhaps the SCSCF is trying to forward
the REGISTER-request without success?

> Hi,
> 
> We've implemented Click-2-Dial (C2D) with OpenIMS + Sailfin without any
> triggers.
> 
> The flow goes something like this (see RFC3725 section 4.1 for more
> details.
> Notice that this flow is not optimal in all scenarios (ref: section
> 5)):
> 
> 1. User (Alice) goes to the website and "clicks to dial to Bob".
> 
> 2. C2D http servlet creates an INVITE request that is sent to Alice.
> 
> 3. Alice receives INVITE request and accepts it.
> 
> 4. C2D sip servlet receives accept from Alice. Creates a new INVITE
> request
> that is sent to Bob.
> 
> 5. Bob receives INVITE request and replies with OK (to C2D).
> 
> 6. C2D now sends ACK back to Bob.
> 
> 7. C2D sends ACK back to Alice, including Bob's answer.
> 
> As you see, there are no triggers necessary here (from IMS) as "plain
> SIP"
> takes care of the routing.
> 
> Best regards,
> Andreas
