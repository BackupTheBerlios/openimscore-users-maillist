Hi Trowa,

> Is there a chance to change the Digest username in SIP header's
> Authorization field?
> I mean to change:
> Digest username ="bob@<domain>"  -------> Digest username="bob"

No. This is not possible. The username in IMS corresponds to the Private
User Identity (IMPI) and that is defined as a NAI (Network Access
Identifier, specified in RFC 2486 [45]). The format of a NAI is
username at operator.com.
