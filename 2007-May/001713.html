Hello Claudio,

The Open IMS Core does not inspect the Content-Type header. The
difference must be somewhere else in the messaging.

The subscription fails probably because by default there is an example
Initial Filter Criteria in the HSS for presence and you most likely you
have no presence server configured and running.
