Yes, it is the standard way as of 24.229. In general, it's not a good
ides to rely on the client's subscription for security issues. Maybe he
manages to exploit some bug and subscribes only partially, or
unsubscribes, etc....

Well, if you don't like it, just change the pcscf.cfg and remove the
P_subscribe().... but then the P-CSCF's registrar will only synchronize
with the one on the S-CSCF through your [de|re]REGISTERs.

-Dragos
