Hi,
when we are talking about AKA and replacement of FoHSS by third party 
HSS, I would like to ask whether OpenIMS Core supports Resynchronization 
procedure (in case of synchronization failure), defined in 33.102 and 
33.203. If xCSCFs do not,  FoHSS cannot  be  replaced by 3GPP compliant 
HSS, because the system would not work :).
Andrej
