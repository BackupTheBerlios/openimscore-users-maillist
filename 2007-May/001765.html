Hello Allesandro,

there is no route(ACK) because it does not make sense to create such a
route when it would be called just once and the contents are just 2 line
long, instead of writing its contents directly where needed. As you can
see for REGISTER there is a whole lot more to do.

The route() is a kind of subroutine call. You can find the Orig_initial
a little bit down, at the route[Orig_Initial].

Then from your trace and log the issues is very puzzling. Could you
please increase the debugging output to 5 in pcscf.cfg and send one more
time the log?

-Dragos
