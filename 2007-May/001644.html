Hi Yogi,

I still could not find anything because I only see the packets in the
ethereal log from User Agent (192.168.1.254) to P-CSCF (192.168.1.1). The
interesting part is what I- and S-CSCF are doing and their traffic with HSS.

You should trace all the other nodes as well including Diameter traffic.
For this I usually use "tcpdump".

tcpdump -i any -s 2000 -w /tmp/trace1.pcap port 3060 or port 4060 or port
5060 or port 6060 or port 3868 or port 3869 or port 3870.

Thgis trace could be the starting point to find the problem.
