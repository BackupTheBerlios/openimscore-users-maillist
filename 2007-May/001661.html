Hello Daniel,

The SIP2IMS Gateway was adding/replacing that Authorization header. The
IMPI there was derived from the IMPU, in the same manner that the
I/S-CSCF does now. So there is no functionality loss due to SIP2IMS'
deletion.

-Dragos
