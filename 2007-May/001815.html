I used your SIPp-script and got the same result as you. But then I checked
RFC 3261 for "sent-by" parameter in the Via header field and found that your
interpretation obviously is wrong. The sent-by field does not have an
explicit parameter-name. It is just the field after the sent-protocol,
separated by LWS

   via-parm = sent-protocol LWS sent-by *( SEMI via-params )

Do you agree?
