Hello Li Wang,

I think that you meant that you want to change open-ims.test to your
domain and not the IP address.

Anyway, both are possible. Changing the IP address also is up to you. If
you want to move the Open IMS Core out of the loopback cage, then you
also need to change all occurrences of 127.0.0.1 to your particular IP.
