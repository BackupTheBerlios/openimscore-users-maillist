the clients and the other entities. I can only see the internal Open IMS
Core traffic.

2. The Request-URI in the BYE should be set to the Contact address of
the other endpoint.

3. There might also be other issues with the dialog identification, but
I can't tell because I don't understand what kind of B2B call are you
making in there without the full trace.
