Hi all,

I continued the tests with IMS communicator and found the following changed
behaviour between the version "IMS-Communicator 070412" and
"IMS-Communicator 070427":

The new version overwrites the parameter value of "SECURITY_PORT_C" to the
value of "PREFERRED_LOCAL_PORT" in ims-communicator.xml. This might cause
the troubles at setup of SA by P-CSCF.

I have attached the relevant xml-files if of interest 
- ims-communicator_070412.xml 
  where the value of "SECURITY_PORT_C value="3063" is left untouched
- ims-communicator_070427.xml
  where the value of "SECURITY_PORT_C value="3063" is always overwritten to
"3061"

I hope that helps to solve my problems.

cheers
franz
