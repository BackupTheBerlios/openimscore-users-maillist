The P-CSCF sends 2 bytes to keep NAT ports open as explained here by
Franz Edler and Dragos

http://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/openimscore-users/2007-January/002044.html

"This is caused by NAT handling in P-CSCF which is enabled by default
(NAT-pings). You can disable it by setting the "NAT_enable" parameter
in pcscf.cfg to zero."
