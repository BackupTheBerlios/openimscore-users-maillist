 I also faced this problem.After registration you should see NOTIFY
message getting exchanged between SCSCF and PCSCF.Otherwise you get the
error mentioned in the mail.Repeat registration(probably 4-5 times)  and
make sure that the NOTIFY message is getting exchanged before attempting
the call.
