Gaurav,

Could you also take a look at your /var/log/syslog when you restart named? Any error messages generated by named should appear there.

From the man pages for resolv.conf:

"      The domain and search keywords are mutually exclusive. If  more  than one instance of these keywords is present, the last instance wins."

Thus, taking a look at the log file will let us know if named has started off correctly.
