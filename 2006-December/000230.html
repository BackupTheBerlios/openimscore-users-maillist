Hello Mario,

First let me apologize if my response seemed ironic. I assure you that 
it was not my intention to do that. Now that I read it again, I think 
that forgot a "?" after the first smiley and you might have 
missunderstood that. I am not a SER expert so to say, and as such I did 
not remembered exactly if tm has dialog support or that was in the 
dialog module. That is what I meant. It is quite hard for me to keep up 
with the SER features as they are hardly documented. I don't know how 
other people are keeping up with that, but I for one have difficulties 
here due to lack of documentation.

Yes, the NOTIFY was recently updated and takes advantage of that. As 
such these are sent on the same dialog.

We need to modify also the s_dialog and p_dialog (in P-CSCF) pretty soon 
to take advantage of this structure, so that the CSCFs could send BYE 
requests if needed.

So definitely your question was not stupid at all and you are completely 
right here. However, please understand that this was a big effort that 
took a lot of our time to get even to this stage. When having to 
implement more than 4 different components, we had to cut corners in 
order to get sooner to a "usable" version that got released. We could 
have waited more, integrated all the new features and so on. But we 
hoped that by releasing it sooner we can get to a better result in less 
time as we would get help from the community during this process. This 
specific item is on the TODO list and if you could spend some time on 
putting dlg_t in the s_dialog and p_dialog now, your help would be most 
welcomed.

-Dragos
