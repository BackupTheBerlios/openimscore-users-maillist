Carlos,

Here at our end, we have always been registering as Bob, Alice or other users using different PCs. We run the OpenIMScore components on a Linux box. Till now, we do not have a Reverse Zone File, and things are working fine. Thus, in my opinion, a Reverse Zone file is not mandatory. I could be wrong, of course.

About your mysql connection:
Failure to connect to mysql using the IP Address clearly points to a mismatch in your privileges table(s).
Please refer to your mysql documentation for granting the correct privileges.
