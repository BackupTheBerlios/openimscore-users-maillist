As you say, the AS needs to deal with it, so it is not something in the
core.

However, when you want to initiate a call from the AS, you have
basically 2 options:

1. Do origination - and as such, simulate a UE. Then you need to fill a
Route header as indicated in the 3rd party REGISTER that we do not have
yet. This is for now almost the same as the Route header from the client.
2. Do termination - and send the request to the domain of the Req-URI -
that would get resolved into a I-CSCF.
