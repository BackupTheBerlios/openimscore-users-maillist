Hi Borut,

First, the sjphone has a strong deja-vu smell - just talked about it
here... if you can still not found your answer, please post a network
dump and configuration files so that we could help you further.

Regarding the diameter traffic - try and capture all the traffic on the
3rd PC, including the local loop-back interface. Be aware that by
default ethereal/wireshark only decodes the traffic on 3868 as Diameter
- you will have to ask for the other ports explicitly.

-Dragos
