Hi,

I am just testing the association of more than one IMPU to one IMPI.
I have created two additional IMPUs in my test-network for bob:
- sip:bob-business at net1.test
- sip:bob-private at net1.test
to the existing IMPU: sip:bob at net1.test

Now I register one IMPU after the other and get as a result unexpected
content of the P-Associated-URI header field.

When registering sip:bob at net1.test the P-Associated-URI in the response
contains: <p:bob at net1.test>
That's o.k.

When registering sip:bob-business at net1.test the P-Associated-URI in the
response contains: <sip:bob-business at net1.test>, <sip:bob-private at net1.test>
That's not correct as sip:bob-private at net1.test is not registered at that
time.

When registering sip:bob-private at net1.test the P-Associated-URI in the
response contains: <sip:bob-private at net1.test> 
That's not correct as now all three IMPUs are registered.

I have included a wireshark tracefile and a SIP scenario.

Any comment on this?

cheers
franz
