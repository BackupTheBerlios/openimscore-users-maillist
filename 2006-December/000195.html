Hi *,

I am counting now over 200 messages on the users list. So it's time to 
clean up the list a little bit so that it will still be manageable for 
people to follow the discussions.

General rules:
- before posting, read the docs, the FAQ, the lists, blah blah blah...
- do your best to describe the problem. Don't be afraid to post logs, 
configuration files, traffic dumps on the cscf/hss mailing lists. This 
will greatly improve the resolve time for your issue.
- avoid sending requests to individual maintainers. I am, for one, 
swamped with work and it bothers me to respond to the same question 
individually to multiple people. If you post to the list, you will get a 
faster response. As more eyes see your question, the response will be 
better and more eyes will review also the response.

Posting Topics:
- All messages specifically related to the CSCFs should go on 
openimscore-cscf at lists.berlios.de
- All messages specifically related to the HSS should go on 
openimscore-hss at lists.berlios.de
- All other messages go on openimscore-users at lists.berlios.de

- The SVN post-commit logs go to each specific list
- The bug-reports and feature-requests unfortunately go to 
openimscore-users at lists.berlios.de (can't fix this yet...)

And now we need your suggestions:
- What do you think about:
    - the lists
    - the website
    - the code
    - the current features
    - new features
- What can we improve? Or what will make your life easier?
- Do we need to split more the mailing lists on specific topics or there 
are already too many?

Hope to see many response on this topic :)

-Dragos
