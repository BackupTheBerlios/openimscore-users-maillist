Hi,

I just found that P-CSCF subscribes again to the registration-event of a
subscriber after successful de-registration of this subscriber (alice).

In the attached pcap-file you can see:
Packet 29: P-CSCF sends 200 OK on REGISTER (expires=0)
Packet 32: P-CSCF sends 200 OK on NOTIFY (registration-event, terminated)
Packet 35, 39, 43, 47: P-CSCF tries to SUBSCRIBE again to registration-event

Packet 51: 480 Temporary Unavailable - Diameter Cx interface failed

This seems to be a bug, isn't it?
