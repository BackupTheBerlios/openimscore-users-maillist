What I see now is that the call no longer involves the I-CSCF.
INVITE is forwarded from P-CSCF directly to the S-CSCF. That is not correct.

There are 2 ways to get a successful call:
- either with a SIP client and sip2ims gateway , or
- with SIPp which you are obviously using. 
  But yohr SIPp scenario has some bugs.

I suggest that you should try at first SIP-client+sip2ims gateway and than
look at the message contents and try to put it in your SIPp scenario.

Unfortunately I do not have a working SIPp scenario now, but I will have it
soon. I am starting to work on it now.
