I am looking now at pjsip and it looks very clean and nice. The list of 
features is impressive! And, as it was all written buy just 1 guy, it is 
consistent. It could be used for the SIP2IMS or very easily for an IMS 
Client - it only requires a GUI (really!!!) and it runs on all kind of 
plaforms (like linux, bsd, windows, windows ce, symbian on the way, etc).

BTW, 2 post-graduate students at the workshop mentioned that they are 
working on an GTK IMS client (you know who you are ;-) ). If you want to 
get it opened, we will most gladly welcome it on openimscore.org. I am 
sure that all here will not mind if it is not final, 100% working, 
nicely-code-formated, etc. We badly need a client and we are willing to 
help you with it. Of course, the invitation is opened towards anybody 
else that is working on something like this.

-Dragos
