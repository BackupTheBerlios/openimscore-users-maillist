Hi Gaurav,

I am not sure what "trusted server" means in IMS specs. It might be that
you are talking about PSI? We do have that ...

Again, as the scope is not commercial (see the big disclaimer on the
first page), such commercial features as strong security on the S-CSCF
have been deferred for later implementation. This means that the S-CSCF
will accept and route any correctly formed message. For now, security is
present, but at the edge of the network only - P-CSCF for visited,
I-CSCF for home, etc.

-Dragos
