hmm.. I am not sure that I understand your question... Yes, they are
written from "scratch" as-in not reusing any other Diameter stack. Both
have been written with the clear goal of Cx/Sh, but the guideline was
RFC 3588. So it did not made any sense to spend effort on developing for
example Diameter-User-Session when that is not used on these 2
interfaces. As such it is not a 100% implementation of the RFC. However,
both stacks are extensible and we will gladly accept any contributions
to them.

-Dragos
