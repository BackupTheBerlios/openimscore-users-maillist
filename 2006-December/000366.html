Hello Sébastien,

the Request-URI does not look good. The port should not be there (you
register to the domain, not to a certain proxy). Then in IMS you need an
authorization header even in the first REGISTER, to indicate the private
identity. There were already some SIPp scenario already posted by Franz
and me on the list - please check those.

-Dragos
