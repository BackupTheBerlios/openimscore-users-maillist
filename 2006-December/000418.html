Hi Gaurav,

seems as if you are missing the entry in the "hibernate.properties" file.

Per default this connection points to:
hibernate.connection.url=jdbc:mysql://127.0.0.1:3306/hssdb

Adjust that to your settings (also the username and password if you use
something different to hss/hss) and re-start the HSS.
