Hi Ben,

just one remark to an alternative solution: the web console that also
does the job (especially if you just want to add one user ;)

Start the HSS and then go to http://localhost:8080 to provision users
over the HSS Web-Interface. Start making an IMS Subscription, add the
IMPI to it an the IMPU, assign the Service Profile to the IMPU and you
are good to go. 

Regards,

Peter
