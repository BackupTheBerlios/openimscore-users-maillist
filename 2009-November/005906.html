Hi,
 
> I found the description in the TS 24.229:
> 
> NOTE:     The usage of the same parameters for authentication of
> non-registration SIP requests requires the storage of these parameters
> during authentication of REGISTER requests, as retrieval of
> authentication vectors is only specified for REGISTER requests.
> 
> So, the S-CSCF doesn't use MAR/MAA and SAR/SAA to HSS during
> authentication of non-REGISTER.

Yes, exactly. The S-CSCF has to store the password received during registration (diameter MAA, AVP: SIP-Authorization) and re-use it autonomously for re-authentication of non-REGISTER requests.
