Hi,

I am trying to setup the UCT-PCC platform and thereby I detected an issue
with Rx-protocol implemented in P-CSCF.
I recognized that the AA Request sent by P-CSCF in case of a registration
(to enable a bearer for signaling traffic) does not contain a
Media-Component-Number AVP.
This AVP is required and has to be set to value 0 in case of AF signaling.
The missing AVP causes an exception in PCRF implementation of UCT.

I have attached a trace-file where you can see in packets 130 (registration
of Alice) and packet 353 (registration of Bob) the mentioned Diameter AA
Request from P-CSCF to the PCRF.

Can anyone confirm my assumption that the Media-Component-Number AVP (with
value 0) should be provided by P-CSCF?

BR
Franz
