Hi Chhay,

First of all: Do you want to make a video-call or really a
video-mulitparty-conference? I think you can only make 1:1 video-calls
with the two clients, no conferences (3 or more users). In order to
make video-conferences with more users, i think you must use an
application-server.
Are you able to make voice-calls between the users? If voice-calls
work, the rest should work as well.
When you are using a debian-based linux (e.g. Ubuntu or Debian), you
may follow this howto:

- http://uctimsclient.berlios.de/openimscore_on_ubuntu_howto.html

Kind regards,
