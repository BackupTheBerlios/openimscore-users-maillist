> attached is the trace, thanks for helping. 

The problem is with your Cisco gateway. It adds a port-number to the
request-URI: 
"INVITE sip:7817689710 at 4mc00ec.com:5060 SIP/2.0"  
Try to eliminate this port-number from the request-URI.

> One more question, what is
> a good presence server i can install, that works with open core ims.

There is an open source solution available as a combination of
http://www.opensips.org/ and http://openxcap.org/, 
but there are of course also commercial solutions available like
http://www.micromethod.com/develop/dz.htm#presence where you can get an
evaluation license.
