Hi Gowtham,

> the invite from the gateway comes in as a sip uri
> (sip:telephone_no at mydomain.com). once the icscf get this invite its
> sends it to the s-cscf, thats where i get the 404 not found. i can
> place a call out through the gateway by using the tel:TN or
> sip:TN at gateway_ip. Will send you a trace once i get to my office.

Then I think it is clear: The PUID "sip:telephone_no at mydomain.com" is
obviously not provisioned.

If you send a trace you should use the command like:
tcpdump -i any -s 3000 -w /var/tmp/tracefile.pcap not port 3306

It also should include the registration of the subscriber with above PUID.
