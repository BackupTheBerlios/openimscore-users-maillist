Hi all,

Fraunhofer FOKUS and Technische Universität Berlin will be hosting the 
upcoming SIP-Router Development Meeting on 02.10.2009. The SIP Router 
project (sip-router.org) is a merge between the Kamailio (OpenSER) and 
SER (iptel.org). Recently we have upgraded our Open IMS Core modules to 
also be usable in the new SIP-Router, which hopefully on near future 
will be our new home and as such we will benefit from the latest updates 
and fixes, OpenSER features and we'll stop maintaining our own fork of 
SER (aka ser_ims).

More details about the meeting can be found here: 
http://sip-router.org/2009/09/14/development-meeting-2009/

If you plan to attend, please register as indicated on the website.


Cheers,
-Dragos
