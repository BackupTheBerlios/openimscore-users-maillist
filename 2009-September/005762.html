Hi Agung,

for just the clients to be handle dually, you'd only need the P-CSCF to 
have a dual IPv6/IPv4 configuration. Then keep in mind that an IPv4-only 
or IPv6-only node can not communicate with the opposite. Then make sure 
you have a coherent DNS configuration. Based on your errors, I suppose 
that you have some trouble with the DNS entries for the icscf/scscf. A 
network trace always helps in seeing where exactly and on which protocol 
is the HSS trying to connect to.

Cheers,
-Dragos
