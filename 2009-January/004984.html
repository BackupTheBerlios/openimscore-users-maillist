Hi Noriko,

I would recommend to always sent the message to an I-CSCF and not to 
hardcode an S-CSCF route.

If you want to do origination, insert a Route: <originating_domain;lr;orig>
If you want to do termination, don't insert anything.

Then just do normal SIP loose routing, based on the first Route header 
or the Request-URI.

Cheers,
-Dragos
