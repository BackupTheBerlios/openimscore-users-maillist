Hello everyone,

We are trying to establish a call between two SIP modems (16503751111
and 16503751112). The request URI is of the user=phone format. Both
UEs register first and then 1112 calls 1111. However, when the INVITE
from 1112 arrives at the pcscf, the pcscf just dumps the INVITE and
does not forward it to the SCSCF. We noticed that the UE does not
insert a ROUTE header with scscf address in the INVITE. To overcome
this, we have configured the pcscf to enforce service route (Variant
2: P_enforce_service_route). On receiving the INVITE from 1112, the
PCSCF logs are as follows: -
