Hi Yang Kai,

We have started with 3GPP Rel.5, but as it evolved, we have many 
features from all 3GPP revisions up to Rel.8. Also there is partly 
support for Cable-Labs Packet Cable 2.0 and ETSI NGN R1/2.

The ftp files are just automated snapshots of the SVN revisions. I would 
suggest to take the latest ones as there are many bug fixed and new 
features in.

Cheers,
-Dragos
