When the public service provider accepts the port, then it is tolerant and
is able to cope with nonsense.

The principle of SIP addressing is that the inbound proxy queries the
location service with the AoR to get the physical address including port (if
required).
Any port added to the AoR is not part of the AoR and will be replaced by the
physical address anyhow.
