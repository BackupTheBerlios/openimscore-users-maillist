Please do not add a port-number to the AoR  in  INVITE
sip:a5 at open-ims.test:5060.
Just without :5060.

Definition of AoR according to RFC 3261:
Address-of-Record: An address-of-record (AOR) is a SIP or SIPS URI that
points to a domain with a location service that can map the URI to another
URI where the user might be available.

The port-number is part of the physical address and is provided by the
location service (S-CSCF in our case).
