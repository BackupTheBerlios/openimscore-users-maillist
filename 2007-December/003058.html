Hi Andrej,

The lite version of OpenIC only supports G.711 and the audio quality is
not that good either, unless used in a lab environment, I would say.
Yet, we think that it is good enough for the testing purposes that it
was meant for - mainly signaling that is.

Sorry, but the better RTP stacks and codecs only are available on the
non-free version.

Cheers,
-Dragos
