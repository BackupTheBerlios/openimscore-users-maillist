No, the P-CSCF actually looks to identify the previously Contact header
saved in the registration in the Via header of the INVITE. We know that
this is a security issue, but it's quite hard to use the 3GPP
recommended IPSec security together with NAT.

Check that your client uses a Via header similar to the Contact saved
during the registration.

-Dragos
