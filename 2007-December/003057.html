Erling Klæboe wrote:
> Hi
>
> We are working on IM Session Mode Application Servers specified by Open 
> Mobile Alliance (OMA).
> On of the SIP methods required there is REFER.
>
> According to RFC 3515, the REFER method could be placed either outside 
> or inside a dialog,
> so we would like that REFER is added as a possible SIP method in IFC TP 
> in OpenIMSCore.
> Guess that should be straight forward ?
>
> A REFER request MAY be placed outside the scope of a dialog created
>   with an INVITE.  REFER creates a dialog, and MAY be Record-Routed,
>   hence MUST contain a single Contact header field value.  REFERs
>   occurring inside an existing dialog MUST follow the Route/Record-
>   Route logic of that dialog.
