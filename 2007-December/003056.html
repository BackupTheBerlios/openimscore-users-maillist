Hi Jingyi,

AMF and OP are operator-set values. So you just choose your own. The SQN
is a sequence number that will be synchronized with the SQN on the
client's ISIM/USIM.

Of course, the authorization headers are each time quite different.
That's because each time a new random value is used.

Cheers,
-Dragos
