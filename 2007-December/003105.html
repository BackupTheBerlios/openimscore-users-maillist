HI,

> When Bob calls Alice the PCSCF responds with message "400-Bad Request-Not
> following indicated service route". 

As the error says, your client obviously does not honour the Service-Route
header field, which is provided to the client during registration. The
client must use the content of the Service-Route header-field as a preloaded
Route in an INVTE request.

> Please could anyone
> help me to solve this problem without  forcing routing into pcscf?

Use an IMS compliant client (e.g. IMS Communicator or OpenIC Lite).
