as far i could find:
  - 3GPP TS 29.328 Sh interface Signalling flows and message contents:

    /3.1    Definitions
    /

        /Transparent data: Data that is understood syntactically but not
        semantically by the HSS. It is data that an AS may store in the
        HSS to support its service logic. One example is data that an AS
        stores in the HSS, using it as a repository.
        /

        /Non-transparent data: Data that is understood both
        syntactically and semantically by the HSS./

The non-escaped part of the data to store in the HSS is not only 
syntactically but also semantically undertood by the HSS.

& &amp
