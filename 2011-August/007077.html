Hi Franz,

Without any port filters the trace file has a lot of other traffic, that may
make things difficult for you. So I ran two seperate traces and captured
them. One is without port filters as you wanted and one is with filters for
CSCF and HSS ports.

With Port Filters:  tcpdump -s 3000 port 4060 or port 5060 or port 6060 or
port 3867 or port 3868 or port 3869 or port 3870 or port 4868 -i any -w
capture_20110815_3.pcap

Without filters: tcpdump -s 3000 port not 22 -i any -w
capture_20110815_4.pcap

Let me know if these traces give you any details. Thanks for all the help.
