>     unSUBSCRIBE

>     initial SUBSCRIBE and well, fails because the user does not exist
>     anymore...

>     >     The Service-Routes are ONLY for initial requests for dialogs
>     or for
>     >     non-dialog methods. For any subsequent requests inside
>     dialogs you
>     >     need
>     >     to use the Record-Routes for that specific dialog, as saved
>     in the
>     >     200
>     >     OK for the initial request (and in reversed order on the
>     >     originating side).
