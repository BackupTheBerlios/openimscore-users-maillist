My IMS core is built on Fedora 6 and working with Openser Presence Module as default AS. Everything works fine on the previous reversion(I forgot the reversion number), such as REGISTER, MESSAGE, PUBLISH and SUBSCRIBE.After upgrading the core last week, my sip client (designed by Jain-1.2-NIST) failed to PUBLISH and SUBSCRIBE requests.1) From the external trace, the sip client keeps receiving PUBLISH request from the IMS core(weird) more than 10 times after sending PUBLISH to the core. 2) From the internal trace, the pcscf forwards received PUBLISH request to the scscf, and then to the AS. At last, 'time out' error is thrown out.3) SCSCF notification shows that the PUBLISH request does forwards to AS for 4 times, which is following by a BUG message "BUG:t_check_status: t_pick_branch failed to get  a final response in MODE_ONFAILURE".Eventually, SCSCF crashes.I attached these traces in this email. They are `external_trace`, `internal_trace` and `pcscf, icscf, scscf and hss notifications`. I extract them in the zip format because of more than 512K. Sorry about that.Waiting for your help!
