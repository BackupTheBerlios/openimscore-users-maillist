Solved.It is the problem of Jain-sip stack outbound_proxy property. Even if the property of outbound proxy is set as 'pcscf.open-ims.test/udp', the route header in the request is also needed for the PUBLISH and SUBSCRIBE request. Otherwise, the messages is sent to scscf. That is why there is no SUBSCRIBE trace in the internal trace.Kevin,
