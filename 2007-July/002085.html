I don't really understand what you mean by "But at this subscription the
Recodr-Route is taken into account and replaced the "Service-Route"".
Could you please send a trace.

Oh, and please be aware that if you un-SUBSCRIBE after the
de-registration, probably the SUBSCRIBE dialog does not exist anymore as
it would've been closed by the last NOTIFY coming from the S-CSCF - see
the subscription-status in the message. As such, un-SUBSCRIBTION on
de-registration is not necessary.
