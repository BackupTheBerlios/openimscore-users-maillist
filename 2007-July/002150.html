When you do a search on the IMPU and you edit sip:alice at ds.jdsu.net
there should be an entry for the IMPI alice at ds.jdsu.net in the "List of
associated IMPIs" section. Otherwise, use the "Associate IMPI(s) to
IMPU" box above.
