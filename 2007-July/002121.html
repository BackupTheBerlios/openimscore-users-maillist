Hello Li,

Please keep in mind that in order for the Open IMS Core to work outside
localhost, you need to change ALL the CSCFs out of localhost IP addresses.

Most probably you forgot the P or I-CSCF. In any case, if still in
trouble, please send a trace.

-Dragos
