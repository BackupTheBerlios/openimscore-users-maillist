The Diameter stacks are used for the Cx and Sh interface communication.
The JavaDiameterPeer is used in the HSS, as the HSS is written in Java.
The CDiameterPeer is used in the CSCFs.

No, there are no CDR sent. Charging in IMS is more or less a task for
Charging Application Servers and the regular CSCF charging interfaces
are not yet fully implemented.
