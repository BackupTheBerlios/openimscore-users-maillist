Do you actually know the OP/AMF/K parameters of your SIM card's AKA
authentication? This info is impossible to be read out of it... so try
to find this from the provider and insert the info in the HSS.

I have already answered to a similar question about disabling
authentication, just moments ago.

Alternatively, you could set the phone on IETF profile and do MD5
authentication. My N95 works just fine like that. The 3GPP profile
didn't work for me because I don't know the parameters above either....
But the AKA was validated and is correct.
