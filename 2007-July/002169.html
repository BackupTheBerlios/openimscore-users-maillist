Hello Kevin,

Well, things have changed, new stuff has been added and we hope that
many improvements have been made. Usually we log a lot on our new
features. When they get relatively stable the logs are removed. Well, in
a way, one of the scopes was to raise awareness on the new stuff added,
so we have succeded.

The new logged lines are the lists of supported application ids and
vendor ids advertised by the respective remote peer.

The new interface is updated to Release 7. We think that it is a move
forwards. We're sorry that we chose not to prioritize the "looks" over
functionality, but we hardly have now resources for beautifying it.
Well, if you think that you can help to improve it, you're welcome to do so.

About localhost - could you please check that the name localhost
resolves on your computer actually into 127.0.0.1 ?

-Dragos
