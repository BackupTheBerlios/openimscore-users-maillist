Hi all,

for some days now we have been working on migrating the website of the
project to a more community friendly version managed with Drupal. After
all, we can only benefit for contributions from you guys ;)

So feel free to check out the preview of the new website under
www.openimscore.org/openimscore (make use of the poll on the right side
as well) and feel free to provide us with feedback on what more features
you would want to see there.

Once you have an account there, you should be able to help us in
improving the content of the site to become even more informative. In
addition, we will also be working on getting some more elements moved
there until the final replacement of the current webpages.

Regards,
