Hello Chen,

Yes, the subscription expiration time is set to the maximum contact
expiration time for that identity + 30 seconds. This is updated on each
re-registration or on RTR events when for example the HSS asks for an
update on the contacts expiration time.

I don't see it as a bug, but as a feature that makes the life of the UE
easier, as you don't have to bother with re-subscriptions during the
registration period as the original subscription will keep being active
for the whole registration period and this extensions are communicated
in the notifications. Do you think that this is bad? If yes, why?

-Dragos
