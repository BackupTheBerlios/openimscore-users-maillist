Sorry for my late answer to this topic, sometimes I am too busy.
I want to mention, that I like to work with VMware and I am quite
enthusiastic about it. It gives the opportunity to run e.g. two different
networks of OpenIMSCore and two instances of IMS communicator on my notebook
while I am still On-Line with WindowsXP. The VMware Player is free and there
is also a possibility to get a free serial number for the VMware Server
(http://www.vmware.com/download/server/).

To get rid of performance issues I use a slim installation of Slackware
11.0. The actually zipped VMware image is actually about 627MB and it
contains a ready to run OpenIMSCore (revision 245) including DNS and SIPp
scenarios.

If there is interest let me know, I would like to contribute VMware images.
I regularly update the images, because I use it also for my students.
