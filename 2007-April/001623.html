Hi Kiran.
For issues regarding IMS-Communicator, please refer to the IMS-Communicator lists instead, since IMS-Communicator and Open IMS Core are distinct projects.
 
About the first problem (Failed to acknowledge call), as you can read in the output, the Exception retrieves the message "java.net.UnknownHostException: pcscf.mkd149633.wipro.com".
This means that the FQDN "pcscf.mkd149633.wipro.com" cannot be resolved by the DNS configured at your client side, making the jain-sip stack to fail when trying to acknowledge call.
 
About the second problem, please check if the SIP phone you are using has any common codec with IMS-Communicator - http://java.sun.com/products/java-media/jmf/2.1.1/formats.html
