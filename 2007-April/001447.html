Then add in your pcscf.cfg at the beginning the following lines:

if (!uri=~"sip:.*" ) {
    sl_send_reply("416","Unsupported URI Scheme");
    exit();
}

But I wouldn't put this in the P-CSCF in order to have it generic. I
mean the P-CSCF usually just fwds the messages. What scheme are you
talking about that the P-CSCF doesn't understand?
