Sure. You will have to edit then the icscf database and also insert the
new one. Define some capabilities for the S-CSCFs and then assign those
to different users.

We are working now on something like having a default auth scheme in the
HSS and the S-CSCF sending "Unknown" and letting the HSS decide. Also,
if you send for example Authorization: .... algorithm=AKAv1-MD5 ... you
will force a AKAv1-MD5 instead of what is the default one. You can do
the same with MD5 or AKAv2-MD5 at the moment, more to come soon.
