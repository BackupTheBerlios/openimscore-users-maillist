Unfortunately SER is not able to make the UDP/TCP decision based just on
the message size. Normally, if you would change your AS SIP URI to also
include a transport=TCP parameter, the S-CSCF should then forward always
over TCP.

So try and change the AS uri to something like
"sip:as.open-ims.test:7060;transport=TCP"
