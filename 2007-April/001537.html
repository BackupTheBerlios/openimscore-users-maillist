Hello Yogi,

No, you shouldn't have modified the configurator.sh script the first
time. The script is looking for the DEFAULTIP in the files that it is
going to modify and replaces them with your input. You should change the
DEFAULTIP only after you ran it once and you want now to change from
your old IP address to a new one.

-Dragos
