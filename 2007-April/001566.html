Hi Josef,

I don't think that there are too many devices yet out there that can
claim fully tested IMS support for the integration of ISIMs, mainly due
to the fact that AFAIK no ISIMs are widely used yet with operators (any
updates on this are welcome ;).

The solutions we know mostly helped themselves by using a software
solution rather than relying on a UICC to do the authentication for
them.

Maybe also worth a look might be
http://www.comneon.com/CDA/ims_client_software,483,0,,en.html
