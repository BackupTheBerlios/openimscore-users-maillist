Hi Gaurav,

Take a look at the REGISTER route in the scscf.cfg and you will find
most of your answers. For example, if your client uses IPSec tunnels and
the P-CSCF can add a integrity-protected=yes parameter to the
Authorization header, then the S-CSCF does not require
re-authentication. Else, it does, as it could be an attack.

-Dragos
