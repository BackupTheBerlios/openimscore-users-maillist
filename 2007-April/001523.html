Hi Allessandro

Sometimes, Wireshark (or ethereal) don't see diameter requests...

So you have to decode TCP requests to Diameter.

For example, one of the port used for Diameter Peer is 3870.
So in your Wireshark trace, do a right clic on frame 229, and choose decode
as...

Then choose in transport the protocole Diameter...
Now you can see every Diameter exchanges...

Do the same for (3869?) I think (between I-CSCF and HSS)
