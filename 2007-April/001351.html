Hello Mike,

the Open IMS Playground is operated here in Berlin as an open test
environment for concepts and ideas around NGNs. We have a mixture of own
prototypes (with the Open IMS Core in the center but not limited to it)
and commercial IMS products of various vendors that are applied in many
international and national R&D and industry projects.

The Open IMS Core is the only Open Source development in that context.
All other requests like access to components or prototypes, project
proposals etc. should be settled via direct communication with
Fraunhofer FOKUS - and yes, the projects will somehow have to cover the
costs that arise for the institute (sorry, but we have to make a living,
too ;). The mail address for those kinds of request is info - at-
open-ims.org

Best regards,

Peter
