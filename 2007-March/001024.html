Hello Alessandro,

It seems that your client sends a Register message even with no expiration value, or with a expiration value of 0. Please change this in your client, or use another client which sets this field properly. Expiration value should be different of 0 if you want to stay registered on the IMS. The rest of signaling and messages are good, so this is the only problem that you have.
