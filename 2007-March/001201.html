To be precise: You have to assign the service profiles to IMS Public ID.

 

@ 2)

The I-CSCF might also come into picture (even if both parties are within the
same domain) if the location of the B-party is not known to the originating
S-CSCF. In this case the request might be forwarded also to a different
S-CSCF within the same domain. We have just only a small network: Bob and
Alice both have been assigned to the same S-CSCF during registration.
Therefore only one S-CSCF and I-SCSF is involved within a domain.
