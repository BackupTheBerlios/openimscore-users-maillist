Hi
 
I have a query about P-charging-vector. Section 5.2.1 of 24.229 says
that 
 
When the P-CSCF receives any request or response from the UE, the P-CSCF
shall:
-     remove the P-Charging-Function-Addresses and P-Charging-Vector
headers, if present. Also, the P-CSCF shall ignore any data received in
the P-Charging-Function-Addresses and P-Charging-Vector headers; and
I don't know the significance of the above statement in the spec. When
P-CSCF has to anyway remove the header, then why should UE send the
P-Charging-Vector header?
 
Anyway going by spec, I tried the following scenario between Alice and
bob. All CSCFs, HSS and SIPp are running on same machine:
 
In default SIPp scripts, I added the following line in the INVITE
message:
P-Charging-Vector: icid-value="kashdklajhfd9uiqw78112127912";
icid-generated-at="10.0.64.17"; orig-ioi="open-ims.test"
 
When message goes to P-CSCF, P-CSCF also adds a P-Charging-Vector header
and now there are two P-Charging-Vector headers in the forwarded
message. Shouldn't P-CSCF remove the P-Charging-Vector header sent by UE
before forwarding the INVITE message as per the spec?
