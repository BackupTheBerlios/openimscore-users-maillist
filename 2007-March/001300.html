Any SIP server/proxy can be called Application Server as the concept is
very generic. And then you actually have one already, you just have to
configure it: SER. You could just build a as.cfg and do your AS logic there.

Then about IFC provisioning, it's a web-interface operation that was
discussed here many times before and the essence is - just use the
buttons and forms in the FHoSS interface to add a new one - you already
have an example for presence server.
