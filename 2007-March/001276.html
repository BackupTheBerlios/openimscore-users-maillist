You don't need to do anything with your Apache 
configuration(httpd.conf). Just copy this link
to your favourite browser
ftp://ftp.berlios.de/pub/openimscore/snapshots/

After the page loads you can download any of IMS
Components by clicking on the appropriate label.

the latest ser_ims is packed as ser_ims2007321.r200.tgz 
the latest HSS is packed as FHoSS2007321.r200.tgz
the latest CDiameterPeer is packed as CDiameterPeer 2007321.r200.tgz
the latest JavaDiameterPeer is packed as
JavaDiameterPeer2007321.r200.tgz

I hope this helps,
Umut
