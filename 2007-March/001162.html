Hi Rajeswari,

The CSCFs are quite verbose for debugging purposes. The scope of the
project is experimentation and we think that this way it is more helpful
for you than having a black box.

The logs are not necessarily errors. Yes, it is somehow wrong that you
do not have an Authorization header because you are not sending any
information related to the IMPI (private identity). The P-CSCF first
tries to do Early-IMS authentication and that fails because you don't
have a sent-by parameter in the first via header.

You forgot to say what went wrong in the end? Your registration was not
successful??

-Dragos
