I have good news for everyone who uses sip2ims and experiences the 401
problem.


Solution: Don't use sip2ims anymore.

1. Configure your SIP client to connect to the P-CSCF on port 4060
directly.

2. In the HSS, navigate to User Profiles / Private Identity /
Authorization Scheme and change this from "Digest-AKAv1-MD5" to
"Digest-MD5".

3. In scscf.cfg, find "MD5". Make sure the line that ends with "MD5" is
active now and the line ending with "AKAv1-MD5" is commented out.

Now you can connect X-Lite or whatever SIP client you like.
