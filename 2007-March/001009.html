Hi all,

After a tedious work of upgrading the base SER for the CSCFs (because I
though that it won't be useful to keep in svn the exact copy of the
original SER base), I managed to upgrade it from 0.10.99-dev53 to
2.1.0-dev1 (Ottendorf and beyond) in r166. Well, at least now we can
hope that future upgrades will be easier to do as we could just do a
merge for the diffs in SER.

You won't see the commit log on the list because it was too big and the
lists rejected that.

Benefits? Lots of fixes in SER!

I tested it in basic scenarios and it seems OK. In case it breaks for
you, please let me know.

Cheers,
Dragos
