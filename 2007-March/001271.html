Hi all.
I found 3 specifications regarding HTTP Digest at Cx.
1) RFC 4740
2) Cable Labs 29.228/9 - it looks like more detailed specification based 
on RFC 4740
3) TS 183 033 extension to 3gpp 29.228

Does someone know, which one will be used in Open IMS in the future as a 
replacement of FOCUS proprietary HTTP digest solution? Thanks.
Andrej
