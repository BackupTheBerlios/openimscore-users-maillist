The same question I asked in December 2006. The answer was: 

The INFO warnings for "Failed finding avp" are OK, no worries. They are
there because if some AVP is missing, it will go unnoticed. However, some
AVPs are optional. CDiameterPeer does not have a list of such optional
AVPs/message and as such the normal cases can not be yet detected and
filtered.

Therfore: Don't worry.
