Hello Gaurav,

One thing is that your re-invite should use as Request-URI the other's
end contact address saved for the dialog in the first INVITE
transaction, sip:gaurav at 10.0.64.18:3062. Because of this, the
terminating P-CSCF won't know where to fwd this INVITE.

Then the To header seems to be invalid - you are missing a "sip:" in
both INVITEs.

Now the actual 403 comes because the P-CSCF does not correctly identify
the Terminating Subsequent case. Could you please fix the above issues
first and try again? Sorry, but I am traveling now and it is hard for me
to do a practical test myself.
