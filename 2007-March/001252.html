How does one define a triggerpoint for "Content/type: text/plain" ?

SIP Header: Content-type
SIP Header Content: text/plain

did not work, nor did '.+plain' or 'text./plain'. Can the Header name stay 
as 'Content-type' or should I add there also some regular expression? and 
how does one write 'text/plain' as a regular exp.?

Thank you for the answers!


> > can someone explain me how it will possible to use wildcards or reg exp
> > in trigger points.in my case all calls which starts with 0800 should be
> > triggered.
>
>Looking into TS 29.228 I have the impression that regular expressions are
>not allowed for the Request-URI component of a SPT.
>
>According to Annex F of TS 29.228 regular expressions are (only) allowed 
>for
>matching
>- Header fields
>- Content of a specific Header
>- Line fields in SDP
>- Content of a specific line in SDP
>
>You can verify my assumption that regular expressions are not interpreted 
>at
>R-URIs by sending the URI as entered "sip:0800.+ at ims-core.berlin.test".
