>>> I think that Asterisk is great! Actually it's one of the big SIP open
>>> source products out there.
>>>
>>> However, I don't think that it is either targeted at handling millions
>>> of users, rather hundreds to thousands. Or for that matter to trigger
>>> services in the IMS sense.
>>>
>>> And here is where our Open IMS Core comes in place - in theory it should
>>> be able to handle all those millions of users starving for services, but
>>> not the services ;-). It does not have all the bells and whistles that
>>> Asterisk has (actually, it's just about signaling and no media), so

>>> would have many advantages.
