Hi Naveen,
 
> I want to integrate Asterisk (as a PBX) with openimscore but have no idea
> of how to go about doing that.

First you have to decide upon the scope of "integration". Do you mean
interconnect (so that users in IMS and Asterisk can setup sessions between
each other) or do you mean re-use of services implemented on Asterisk within
IMS (Asterisk as an IMS application server)?

The first way (interconnect) should not be a big deal.

The second way (Asterisk as an IMS AS) requires more tricks, because
Asterisk has not been designed to act as an AS for IMS. There were already
some discussions on the mailing list on that issue. I think that in this
case it could be necessary to put a SER instance in between to do necessary
adaptations in some cases.
