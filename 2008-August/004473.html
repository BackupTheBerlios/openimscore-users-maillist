Hi,

 

according to your log below there are two issues as you marked:

 

1. You need a diameter connection between S-CSCF and the HSS.

    It is useless to try to register as long as there are no diameter
connections active.

 

2. Your Public and/or Private User Identity in REGISTER request is wrong (I
guess it’s the username in Authorization header field).

     To be precise a wireshark/tcpdump trace would be necessary.

 

-franz
