Hi,

from my experience, Kapanga is not fully working with OpenIMS because it doesn't
take into account the service-route header in 200_OK of REGISTER.

So Invite are rejected with 400_BAdRequest or no response if pcscf.cfg is
modified  to enforce route (which is may be a bug...??)
So, with Kapanga, you can only receive calls !

Personally, I user X-Lite (freeware from Counterpath), and do calls from X-Lite
to Kapanga only.

you can also use UCT client.
