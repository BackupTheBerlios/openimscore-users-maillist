Hi Veena,

the behavior of your client at registration seems somehow odd...

first Register: client uses 8080 in via and contact header, although it mentions in the security-client 4060 (shouldn't be a big deal, but still it's not really nice).
second register: 4060 in via and contact header; so far so good. but it uses tcp port 37886 instead of 4060 as source port, i.e. it bypasses the (most probably already created) IPSec SAs.

as far as I understand the trace right your ipsec-client is then called by another party and acts correct... did you try to send an INVITE from the registrated ipsec-client?
