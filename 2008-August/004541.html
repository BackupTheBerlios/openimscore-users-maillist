Both CDiameterPeer and JavaDiameterPeer stacks can be used for any of the interfaces. Actually, CDiameterPeer is a little more advanced as there is already in development a version of the Authorization/Accounting State Machines (but for Cx/Sh you don't need it).

Unlike other Diameter stacks, ours are quite lightweight and don't have "profiles" pre-defined for specific interfaces.

Then about CDiameterPeer-Sh, there were several discussions in the past on adding this capabilities into SER, as to make it a fully featured IMS AS.
