Found the culprit through the "netstat -utanp" command.  It is 'UCT IMS
Client'.  It occupies udp "0.0.0.0:5060" and "0.0.0.0:5061".  As long as
I start "./icscf.sh" first (to be the first to occupy udp port 5060),
before I start the 'UCT IMS Client', everything will work.  The other
way around does not work.
