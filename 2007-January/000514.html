But Bindu, the headers received and sent by the SIP2IMS are completely
different on the 2 sides. There are 2 authorization happening:
- one between the SIP client and the SIP2IMS gateway as a server - that
is MD5
- one between the SIP2IMS gateway as a client and the IMS Core as a
server - that is AKAv1-MD5

They happen in "parallel" and if the first is successful, the the
SIP2IMS gateway will try and make the 2nd one successful.

Regarding authorization with an AKA client, there was recent fix. Please
see the revision 84 log:
https://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/openimscore-cscf/2007-January/000121.html

-Dragos
