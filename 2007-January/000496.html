Hi all,

As you have probably noticed, the berlios.de portal and openimscore.org
have been in the last months sporadically unreachable. The reason for
this are DoS attacks and although the berlios engineers are trying hard
to find a filtering solution, there was not much success so far. Also,
FOKUS donated already some hardware to berlios to help you have a better
experience.

In the meantime, I would like to suggest a workaround: the https seems
to work better during attacks - so use https://www.openimscore.org and
https://developer.berlios.de/projects/openimscore/. Also, it seems that
the SVN access is not affected. I hope that soon we will have some
mirrors too.

-Dragos
