Hi,

i have the following problem:

Assume that a client has registered sucessfully e.g. as <bob at 10.10.10.1:3533>

This client sends then an INVITE message, which has as Via header a different 
Port than in its Contact header:

Via: SIP/2.0/TCP 10.10.10.1:5060
Contact: <sip:bob at 10.10.10.1:3533>

The P-CSCF declines this request:

403 Forbidden - Not Registered! You must register first with a S-CSCF


The P-CSCF searches for the user <bob at 10.10.10.1:5060>:

 5(23007) DBG:P-CSCF:P_is_registered: Looking if registered
 5(23007) DBG:P-CSCF:P_is_registered: Looking for <2://10.10.10.1:5060>

But he is actually registered as <bob at 10.10.10.1:3533> (!!)

So i think the P-CSCF searches for this user by means of the Via-header.
This fails if the port of the Via-header is different than the Contact-header 
port.

Is this a bug of the client?
