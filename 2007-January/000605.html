I think that the problem is in the 2nd REGISTER that comes without an
authorization header.

The example in our wiki:
http://openfacts.berlios.de/index-en.phtml?title=Open_IMS_Core_SIPp_registration
is different:

<![CDATA[
REGISTER sip:open-ims.test SIP/2.0
Via: SIP/2.0/[transport] [local_ip]:[local_port];branch=[branch]
From: "[field0]" <sip:[field0]@open-ims.test>;tag=[call_number]
To: "[field0]" <sip:[field0]@open-ims.test>
Call-ID: [call_id]
CSeq: 4 REGISTER
Content-Length: 0
Contact: <sip:[field0]@[local_ip]:[local_port]>;expires=0
[field2]
Require: sec-agree

]]>
