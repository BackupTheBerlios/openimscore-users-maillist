Hi Bindu,

the key generation is totally up to you since this key is the equivalent of what is stored within the ISIM for the IMPI - so you can put a random key there. The operator ID is used in the process of keying material generation for encryption on the first hop to the P-CSCF, just be sure that the changes you make here arre also reflected in the client implementation (resp. in the SIP2IMS GW if you use it).

- Peter
