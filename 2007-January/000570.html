The strangest thing in my opinion is that after the SL75 sets that big
Contact, when someone calls it, it responds with 404... so it does not
recognize itself... As such, I don't know what could we do in the core
to elevate your problem...

Setting the From/To headers without domain is also wrong as those are
the public identities... It seems that the guys that wrote that UA 
interpreted RFC3261 as the wanted...
