O.k. you are also right, I did not see it.
In this case it would help to see the log-file of P-CSCF.

To exactly mark the position when a request comes in I add a trace statement
at the beginning of the main-route block:

	route{
	xlog("L_INFO","%CybFE: %cs %rm %ru from %si:%sp From: %fu  To:
%tu\n%Cxx");
	route(Sanity_Checks);

This helps to find the arrival of a request quickly within the log-file.
Of course you also have to add a loadmodule statement for xlog:
	loadmodule "/opt/OpenIMSCore/ser_ims/modules/xlog/xlog.so"
