Hi Francesco,

> After registration and a SUBSCRIBE message (sended from the client), I-
> CSCF
> sends another three 'REGISTER' messages to S-CSCF with the same Cseq of
> the
> first (unauthorized) registration message. I don't know if it is its
> normal
> behaviour.

This are retransmissions, a strange phenomenon which I detected also long
time ago but did not follow due to limited time. 

> After some SUBSCRIBE and NOTIFY messages (most of them between P and S
> nodes
> with no interaction with the client), P-CSCF reply with a '500 ERROR' to a
> NOTIFY message. I don't know where that error message come from. 

This indeed is a more serious problem why P-CSCF rejects the NOTIFY to the
reg-Event (500 Error encountered while processing notification).
To get more insight in the problem you should look at the P-CSCF log.
Usually there are some error-lines which give further hints. If this is not
sufficient you should increase Debug-Level to 4, but then the log is very
verbose.

If you need assistance provide the log and the trace.
