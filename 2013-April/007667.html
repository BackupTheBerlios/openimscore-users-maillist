Hi Ravindra,

 

That's easy. IMS requires all clients to follow for new standalone or dialog
initiating requests  the "Service Route" received during registration (just
read a little bit about IMS).

Your clients obviously are no IMS clients and I guess they do not support
the Service-Route header field.

 

To overcome this issue just switch to "variant 2" in pcscf.cfg

                if (!P_follows_service_routes()){


                                #Variant 1 - deny access to the network

                                sl_send_reply("400","Bad Request - Not
following indicated Service-Routes");                      

                                break;

                                #Variant 2 - enforce routes and let the
dialog continue

                                #P_enforce_service_routes();

then it will work.

BR Franz
