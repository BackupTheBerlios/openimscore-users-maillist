> Are there some diagnostics beyond those at the bottom of my original e-
> mail that would help to discover the cause of the crash?  Or other
> scenarios I should try?

If the crash can be reproduced you should offer a backtrace protocol of the
core-dump file (by help of "gdb"). The core-dump is usually in the /root
directory. You should follow the steps:
	- load the GNU debugger (gdb)
	- from /opt/OpenIMSCore/ser_ims start the commands:
        gdb ser /core
	  bt
	  q

In addition you should provide a signalling trace and a log-file. Then the
experts may have a look on the issue.
