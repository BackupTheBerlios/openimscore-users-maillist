Hi Ion,

> I-CSCF doesn't rejects the REGISTER, it forwards the 401 received from
S-CSCF.

Yes, I see. Unfortunately the S-CSCF and Diameter messages are not in the
trace. This caused my wrong conclusion.

I meanwhile have checked the background of " 403","Forbidden - Not
Registered! You must register first with a S-CSCF" and found the reason of
your problem:
It is NAT. The P-CSCF checks the Via-header (transport, IP and port) of e.g.
the SUBSCRIBE request and recognizes a mismatch with registered data (look
at the P-CSCF console, where the registration data are shown periodically).
