Hi Franz,

I-CSCF doesn't rejects the REGISTER, it forwards the 401 received from S-CSCF.

My REGISTER flow looks like:
1. UE sends REGISTER to P-CSCF
2. P-CSCF forwards Register message to I-CSCF, obtained from DNS response;
3. I-CSCF query the HSS to assigned S-CSCF;
4. I-CSCF forwards Register to selected S-CSCF
5. Because UE is not authenticated, S-CSCF sends 401 Unauthorized to I-CSCF;
6. ICSCF forwards 401 to P-CSCF
7. P-CSCF forwards 401 to UE.
