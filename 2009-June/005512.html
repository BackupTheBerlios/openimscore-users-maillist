Hello Kavitha,

it looks like you configured the S-CSCF to listen only on IPv6 and then 
try to use IPv4. You should add also 127.0.0.1 address as a listening 
point, or otherwise SER does not have an interface to send the messages 
towards your PSTN gateway. Or, alternatively, you could move your PSTN 
gateway to IPv6 and forward to ::1.

Cheers,
-Dragos
