2009/5/29 Björn Persson <bjorn.persson at softwell.se>:
> I had a problem with missing IPsec security associations. It turned out that
> big SPIs were misrepresented as negative numbers, causing syntax errors in
> the commands to Setkey. This patch changes the datatype used for SPIs to one
> that is more likely to have room for numbers up to and including 4294967295.
> It also makes the types used for port numbers a bit more consistent.
