Pedro,

the situation seems to be clear.
You client does not follow the protocol rules of IMS and it does not insert
the appropriate Route header field in INVITE as requested by the
"Service-Route" header field of 200 OK response to REGISTER.
You now have 2 possibilities:
1) tell your client to do that
2) remove the check on "Service-Route" in pcscf.cfg (see line 430 ...
Variant 2)
