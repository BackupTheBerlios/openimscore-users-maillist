> response, Proxy-Authentication-Info header in Non-REGISTER response.

I guess you are referring  to 3GPP TS 33.203 Annex N (Enhancements to the
access security to enable SIP Digest).
AFAIK this enhancement of Release 8 (in particular the SD-AV) has not been
implemented in OpenIMSCore yet.

But there is always room for improvement
