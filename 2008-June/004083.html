Hi,

 

The PCSCF requires that the client authenticates and authorizes itself. To
accomplish that you should reply with a new REGISTER-request (in the same
dialog!) that has a WWW-Authenticate header.

 

If you look at the 401 response there should be a WWW-Authenticate header as
well. The header included in this new REGISTER-request should answer that
one.

 

See section 5.2 of 3GPP TS 23.228 for an overview (stage 2) and also 3GPP TS
24.229 section 5.1.1 for the details (stage 3).

 

Btw: I wonder why you choose to develop your own SIP stack from scratch
instead of reusing an existing one (e.g., Sofia-SIP Library
(http://sofia-sip.sourceforge.net/) and others available at
http://www.pernau.at/kd/voip/bookmarks-sip-stacks.html) ?
