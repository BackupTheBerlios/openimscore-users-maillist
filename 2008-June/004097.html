Hi Louis,
 
> my guess is that the P-CSCF in this flow is expecting the RURI, NOTIFY
> alice at open-ims.test, and since it is not (i.e. thinks the NOTIFY is not
> for the local domain), it gets routed to the I-CSCF, is this correct?

No. P-CSCF should accept the Contact-URI as RURI, but it obviously does not
fit into the IMS routing rules of P-CSCF.

I suggest that you provide a P-CSCF log in that case. To easier identify the
requests routed by P-CSCF you should insert a trace-point at the beginning
of the route-block like:
xlog("L_INFO","%Cyb: %cs %rm %ru from %si:%sp From: %fu  To: %tu\n%Cxx");
