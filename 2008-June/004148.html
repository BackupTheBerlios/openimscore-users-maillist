Maybe my comments can help:

There are 2 models as you said
- IPTV outside of IMS, but within the NGN architecture, as a dedicated
non-IMS based subsystem
- IPTV as an IMS application

Operators have the choice to choose between both models. Usually
IPTV-operators have a non-IMS based IPTV-infrastructure in place (early
mover advantage). In this case the operator may migrate towards an IMS based
solution in future. This depends on
- Availability of an IMS infrastructure
- IPTV platform vendor
- Commercial aspects
