Hm... interesting. This throws off all the normal registrar 
optimizations and could be quite bad for the performance of the S-CSCF, 
just like wildcarded PSI are and maybe worse as one can not split into 
S-CSCFs for them and for the others. I wouldn't reject a patch for 
supporting this, but I would wait a bit... this might be just another 
fluke introduced by someone that does not have a big picture of IMS, 
like the orig/term parameters for the ISC was...
