Hi Christelle,

hmm... I think that you have actually the following configuration:

@ i-cscf:
	- Mw interface (SIP) 172.23.10.54:5060
	- Cx interface (Diameter) 172.23.10.54:x
@ scscf:
	- Mw interface (SIP) 172.23.10.*55*:6060
	- Cx interface (Diameter) 172.23.10.*54*:y

So you just misinterpreted the traces. You probably would wan to move 
all the S-CSCF interface on .55, just to avoid such confusions... (edit 
scscf.xml for the acceptor binded address). Your HSS probably fails to 
connect to the S-CSCF, but this worked because the S-CSCF initiated the 
connection.
