Hi Arnaud,
 
> Is someone may explain to me how to manage the logs of the different CSCFs

What do you mean with "manage"?
If you mean "read and understand" then you should walk through the
xcscf.cfg-files and look at the log-statements there like "log(1,">>
Orig_Initial\n");" There are sometimes also additional log-statements in the
code.

To better see how a particular request is handled I additionally add a
tracepoint at the beginning of the route-block like:
xlog("L_INFO","%Cyb: %cs %rm %ru from %si:%sp From: %fu  To: %tu\n%Cxx");
That should help.
