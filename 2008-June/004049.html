Hi Kevin,

No clue about why it rejects the NOTIFY - try searching on the N95 logs.

Then the SUBSCRIBE has several errors:
- It should be Route and not Router the header names there
- Not :lr, but ;lr because lr (loose route) is a parameter, not a port
number
- you should route your messages through the P-CSCF, not directly to the
I-CSCF... if you fix the previous points, this will probably also be
fixed, but you should check anyway.

Cheers,
-Dragos
