But why overwrite? You can explicitly expire a contact that you no 
longer need, even from another contact that itself. In this way you 
could have a nice overlap, which will allow you to orchestrate a nice 
handover between sessions, without breaking connections, and at the end 
from either contact, deregister the one that you don't need anymore.

What you still need is probably a way for the AN to inform your P-CSCF 
in case you lost connectivity and the P-CSCF to de-register your 
contacts on itself... But this is not specified, so maybe I am missing 
something and this should be done some other way...

-Dragos
