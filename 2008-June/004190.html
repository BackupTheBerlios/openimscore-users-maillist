After a first analysis I see that the NOTIFY request after subscription is
returned by P-CSCF to I-CSCF. To get information why the P-CSCF does that I
suggest that you insert a tracepoint in pcscf.cfg at the begin of the main
route-block like:
xlog("L_INFO","%CybTracepoint: %cs %rm %ru from %si:%sp From: %fu  To:
%tu\n%Cxx");

Then look at the log (or send me the log) of P-CSCF further for debug
outputs. How is this NOTIFY handled by the P-CSCF?
