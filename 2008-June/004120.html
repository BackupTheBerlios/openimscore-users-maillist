So it is possible to use the entire or only parts of the project on 
either loopback or external interfaces. However, you can't mix them 
inside the same interface: you either have the communication between the 
HSS and I-CSCF (Cx interface) on loopback or outside.

Technically, even if you could send a packet from 127.0.0.1 to an 
external IP, a response can't come back to you, because the target 
computer has its own notion of the 127.0.0.1 ;-).
