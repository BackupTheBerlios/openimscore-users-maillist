Hi,

This sounds feasible to me, although I don't have much experience with
Asterisk. I think it depends mostly on what kind of info you need on the
Asterisk-side - and if that info is available to your AS.

NB! Currently Sailfin does not support Diameter (for the Sh interface),
which you may need to completely support Asterisk. They plan to implement
such support by Q3 this year (see
http://www.nabble.com/Re%3A-Support-for-Diameter-and-Sh-interfaces-td1347040
0.html#a13471806).
