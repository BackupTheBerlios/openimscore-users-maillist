> Hi Gayathri,
>
> The HSS does not store the IP address that the client used for
> registration, only the name of the particular S-CSCF, which in turn
> saved the Contact address. You can not individually delete one contact
> from an administration interface, but your client should de-register
> them. Of course, an S-CSCF restart will drop all contacts, but this is
> kind of a radical thing ;-).
>
> As Peter said, in case of Early-IMS, you can store the "authorized" IP
> address of the client. It will be stored in the hss_db database impi
> table. But this is used only during the authorization part of the
> registration.
>
> Cheers,
> -Dragos
